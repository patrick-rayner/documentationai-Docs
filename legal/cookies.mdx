**Last updated:** April 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Inkryptus Investimentos e Corretagem Ltda uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit [inkryptus.com](https://inkryptus.com), our mobile applications (iOS/Android), or any other platform, page, or content that we own or operate (collectively, the "Services").

For details about our broader privacy practices, see the [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

## 1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit certain websites or use digital services. They allow us to recognize your device, retain preferences, enhance your browsing experience, and ensure that essential features operate correctly.

Cookies may be set directly by Inkryptus ("first-party cookies") or by third parties providing analytics, content, or advertising services ("third-party cookies").

In addition to cookies, we may use tracking pixels, SDKs (software development kits within our mobile applications), and code snippets that interact with external tools to record online behavior. For simplicity, this document refers to all such technologies collectively as "cookies."

## 2. How we use cookies

Inkryptus employs cookies for the following purposes:

- Ensuring the technical operation and security of the Services.
- Remembering user preferences (for example, selected language).
- Analyzing how users interact with the Services.
- Measuring performance and improving usability.
- Personalizing marketing campaigns and displaying relevant content.

## 3. Categories of cookies

| Category | Purpose | Examples |
|----------|---------|---------|
| **Necessary** | Essential for website operation, account registration, and secure login. Cannot be disabled. | Session management, CSRF protection, authentication |
| **Functional** | Store user preferences such as language and region. | Language selection, display settings |
| **Analytical** | Collect aggregated information on usage of the Services (pages visited, session duration). | Google Analytics |
| **Performance** | Monitor system stability, speed, and efficiency. | Load time tracking, error monitoring |
| **Advertising** | Track browsing activity to deliver personalized ads, measure campaign effectiveness, and limit ad repetition. | Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel |

## 4. How to manage or disable cookies

You can control or disable cookies in the following ways:

**Cookie Preference Center.** Available on our website, where you may adjust your consent at any time.

**Browser settings.** Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or receive alerts about cookies. Disabling cookies may affect the proper functioning of the Services.

**Industry tools.** You may opt out of interest-based advertising via the [Digital Advertising Alliance](https://www.youradchoices.com/).

**Directly with providers.** Some third-party service providers offer their own opt-out options (see section 5 below).
