## 1. Scope and definitions

**1.1** This Schedule ("IBO Terms") establishes the rights, obligations, and rules applicable to Users who choose to participate in the Inkryptus IBO Program (Partner) as Independent Business Owners ("IBOs").

**1.2** The IBO Program is a partnership program in which IBOs promote the Inkryptus platform and its products, and receive commissions based on the real activity generated by their referral network, as described in this document and in the [Commissions](/partner-program/commissions) documentation.

**1.3** Key definitions for this Schedule:

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **IBO** | Independent Business Owner. A User enrolled in the IBO Program. |
| **Network** | The referral structure connected to an IBO, organized in up to 7 levels of depth. |
| **Sponsor** | The IBO whose referral link was used by a new User at registration. |
| **IBO Wallet** | The dedicated wallet within the IBO dashboard where commissions are credited. |
| **Subscription** | The monthly fee required to maintain active IBO status. |

## 2. Enrollment and activation

**2.1** Any registered Inkryptus User may apply to become an IBO by accessing the IBO Program section in the application, reviewing these IBO Terms, and accepting them.

**2.2** Enrollment grants a **30-day free trial** during which the IBO has full access to the dashboard, network tracking, and commission eligibility.

**2.3** After the trial period, continued participation requires an active Subscription of **US$24 per month**, payable in USDT through the application dashboard.

**2.4** Renewal is manual. Automatic renewal may be introduced in a future version of the platform.

**2.5** Payment in USDT was chosen to facilitate access for participants who do not yet hold INKY.

## 3. IBO status and Subscription

**3.1** The IBO status remains active as long as the Subscription is current. No minimum network activity or volume is required beyond maintaining the Subscription.

**3.2** If the Subscription lapses, the IBO status becomes **inactive**. While inactive:

- The IBO ceases to receive commissions from network activity.
- New referrals are not added to the IBO's network.
- The existing network structure is preserved.

**3.3** The IBO may reactivate at any time by resuming Subscription payments. Upon reactivation, the network structure is restored and commissions resume from the date of reactivation. Commissions generated during the inactive period are not retroactive.

## 4. Commissions and payments

**4.1** Commissions are derived from the platform's decision to share a portion of its operational fees with the IBO network. The standard distribution is:

- **30%** of each fee is retained by Inkryptus (operations, infrastructure, liquidity).
- **70%** of each fee is distributed across the IBO network, up to 7 levels (10% per level).

**4.2** When a level has no active IBO (absence of upline), the corresponding share is retained by Inkryptus and may be used for promotional campaigns.

**4.3** Commissions are credited daily to the IBO Wallet in INKY. The IBO Wallet is divided into two sub-wallets:

| Sub-wallet | Receives | Transfer to User Wallet |
|------------|----------|------------------------|
| **Harvest** | Performance fee commissions (staking) | US$3 per harvest operation |
| **Transfer** | All other commissions (subscription, transaction, Arena) | No fee, immediate |

**4.4** To receive performance fee commissions (staking), the IBO must maintain an active staking position in the same asset from which the commission originates. IBOs who do not hold active staking in the relevant asset are not eligible for performance fee commissions on that asset.

**4.5** For detailed fee tables, distribution examples, and commission calculations, see [Fees & Limits](/legal/fees) and [Commissions](/partner-program/commissions).

**4.6** Inkryptus reserves the right to modify commission rates, distribution rules, or fee structures at any time. Changes will be communicated through the application and take effect upon publication.

## 5. Sponsor and network rules

**5.1** Each IBO receives a unique referral link. Users who register through this link are placed at **Level 1** of the IBO's network. Referrals made by those users form Level 2, and so on, up to Level 7.

**5.2** Users who register without a referral link have **30 days** from account creation to manually add a Sponsor via `Profile > Account > Sponsor`. After this window, no Sponsor can be assigned to that account.

**5.3** For a new user to be effectively added to an IBO's network, the IBO must have an **active Subscription** at the time of the referral.

**5.4** Commissions generated before a Sponsor is assigned are not retroactive. Unassigned commissions are retained by Inkryptus.

**5.5** The Sponsor's **username** is always visible to the referred user. The IBO may optionally share additional contact information (name, email, phone) via `Profile > Account > Sponsor`.

**5.6** Sponsorship line integrity is protected. The following actions are expressly prohibited:

- Soliciting, recruiting, or attempting to attract IBOs who are already part of another IBO's network.
- Inducing or facilitating the migration of users or IBOs from one sponsorship line to another.
- Interfering with the relationship between an IBO and their upline or downline members.

Violations of this clause are treated as severe misconduct and may result in suspension or termination of IBO status, as described in Section 10.

## 6. Use of the Inkryptus brand

**6.1** IBOs are authorized to use the Inkryptus name and logo in promotional materials related to the IBO Program, subject to the following conditions:

- The logo and name must be used without modification, distortion, or alteration of colors or proportions.
- Brand materials must not be associated with content that is misleading, offensive, illegal, or that could damage the reputation of Inkryptus.
- The IBO must not create the impression of being an employee, officer, or official representative of Inkryptus. All communications must make clear that the IBO is an independent partner.
- Inkryptus may provide official brand assets and guidelines. When provided, these must be followed.

**6.2** Inkryptus reserves the right to request the removal of any material that violates these guidelines or that, in its reasonable judgment, could harm the platform's reputation.

## 7. Exclusivity

**7.1** While enrolled in the IBO Program, the IBO may not simultaneously act as a partner, affiliate, ambassador, or equivalent role for competing cryptocurrency platforms.

**7.2** A competing platform is defined as any platform that offers substantially similar products (custodial crypto wallets, staking, token swaps, or referral-based commission programs) on the same or comparable blockchain networks.

**7.3** Violation of this exclusivity clause may result in suspension or termination of IBO status, as described in Section 10.

## 8. Conduct and compliance

**8.1** The IBO must conduct all promotional activities in an ethical, transparent, and legally compliant manner. The following are expressly prohibited:

- Promising guaranteed returns, fixed income, or risk-free investment.
- Concealing or minimizing the risks inherent to cryptocurrency investments.
- Using misleading, deceptive, or exaggerated marketing materials.
- Creating multiple accounts to self-benefit from the commission structure.
- Sending unsolicited bulk messages (spam) to promote the platform or the referral link.
- Using paid advertising that impersonates official Inkryptus communications.

**8.2** All promotional content must be based on publicly available and verifiable data (application interface, BscScan, official documentation).

**8.3** The IBO is solely responsible for ensuring that their promotional activities comply with the laws and regulations of their jurisdiction, including advertising standards, consumer protection rules, and data protection regulations.

**8.4** The IBO may choose their own communication methods, channels, and strategies. However, the IBO must never provide information that misleads clients or contradicts official Inkryptus materials. The sole source of truth for product details, fees, commission structures, and platform mechanics is the content published through official Inkryptus channels (application, documentation portal, and official social media accounts). Clients with questions or doubts must be directed to the official support channel at [support@inkryptus.com](mailto:support@inkryptus.com).

**8.5** Providing inaccurate, fabricated, or unauthorized information to clients or prospects, whether intentionally or through negligence, constitutes a serious violation of these IBO Terms and may result in immediate suspension or termination of IBO status, as described in Section 10.

**8.6** The IBO acknowledges that Inkryptus may monitor promotional materials and communications associated with the IBO Program and may request corrections or removal of non-compliant content.

## 9. Tax obligations

**9.1** All commissions and rewards earned through the IBO Program are credited directly to the IBO's Inkryptus wallet. Inkryptus does not withhold, deduct, or report taxes on behalf of IBOs.

**9.2** The IBO is solely responsible for understanding and complying with all tax obligations applicable in their jurisdiction, including reporting income, paying taxes on commission earnings, and maintaining appropriate records.

**9.3** Account Reports for tax purposes can be generated via `Profile > Account > Statement`.

## 10. Suspension and termination

**10.1** Inkryptus may suspend or terminate IBO status when reasonably justified, including but not limited to:

- Violation of these IBO Terms or the [Terms of Use](/legal/terms).
- Breach of the exclusivity clause (Section 7).
- Fraudulent activity, multi-account abuse, or manipulation of the commission structure.
- Misleading or non-compliant promotional conduct.
- Failure to maintain an active Subscription (resulting in inactivity, not termination).

**10.2** In cases of suspension, the IBO will be notified and given the opportunity to provide clarification before permanent action is taken, except in urgent cases involving fraud, legal requirements, or imminent harm to the platform or other users.

**10.3** Upon termination:

- The IBO ceases to receive commissions.
- Earned commissions that have already been credited to the IBO Wallet remain available for withdrawal, subject to the standard withdrawal rules and limits.
- The network structure is preserved but inactive. The terminated IBO cannot reactivate unless the termination is reversed by Inkryptus.

**10.4** The IBO may voluntarily exit the program at any time by discontinuing the Subscription. The network structure is preserved and can be reactivated by resuming payments.

## 11. Independent contractor status

**11.1** The IBO is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, or legal representative of Inkryptus. Nothing in these IBO Terms creates an employment relationship, partnership, joint venture, or agency arrangement between the IBO and Inkryptus.

**11.2** The IBO has no authority to bind Inkryptus, make commitments on its behalf, or represent that they have such authority.

**11.3** The IBO is responsible for their own expenses, equipment, and operational costs related to their participation in the program.

## 12. Limitation of liability

**12.1** Inkryptus is not liable for any losses, damages, or claims arising from the IBO's promotional activities, business decisions, or interactions with their network.

**12.2** The IBO acknowledges that commission earnings depend on the real activity of their network and that Inkryptus makes no guarantees regarding income levels, network growth, or commission amounts.

**12.3** The general limitation of liability provisions in the [Terms of Use](/legal/terms) apply to IBO activities.

## 13. Amendments

**13.1** Inkryptus may amend these IBO Terms at any time. Amendments will be published in the application and on the documentation portal with an updated effective date.

**13.2** Continued participation in the IBO Program after the publication of amendments constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
