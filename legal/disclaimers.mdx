## 1. Nature of digital assets

Cryptocurrencies and digital tokens are not legal tender in most jurisdictions. They are not backed by any government, central bank, or physical commodity. The value of any digital asset, including INKY, depends entirely on market supply and demand and may reach zero.

## 2. Price volatility

Digital asset prices can fluctuate significantly within short periods due to market sentiment, exchange listings, regulatory announcements, macroeconomic conditions, or technical events. Users may lose part or all of their invested capital. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

## 3. Staking and yield

The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) displayed in the Inkryptus application is an informational projection based on the current daily yield. It is not a guarantee of future returns. Factors that affect realized yield include total staked volume across the platform, changes to emission parameters in the staking contract, market price of the rewarded asset, and regulatory or operational changes affecting product availability.

Only rewards that have been claimed (harvested) constitute effective return. A 25% performance fee applies to staking profits at the time of harvest.

## 4. Custodial model

Inkryptus operates as a custodian of user funds. Users do not hold private keys or seed phrases. This means account security, fund availability, and asset recovery depend on the continued operation and integrity of the platform. While Inkryptus employs multisig wallet architecture and layered security controls, no custodial arrangement is immune to all risks.

For details on the security model, see [Security & Verification](/introduction/security).

## 5. Smart contract risk

Platform operations (staking, token transfers, games) rely on smart contracts deployed on BNB Smart Chain. All contracts are public and verifiable on BscScan, but no formal third-party audit by a recognized firm has been completed to date. Undiscovered bugs, logical errors, or vulnerabilities in contract code may result in loss of funds. Users should review contract code and third-party scanner results before committing significant capital.

For contract addresses and scanner results, see [Contracts](/inky-token/contracts) and [Security](/inky-token/security).

## 6. Blockchain and network risk

All transactions on BNB Smart Chain are irreversible once confirmed. Sending assets to the wrong address, on the wrong network, or to an incompatible contract may result in permanent loss. Inkryptus cannot reverse on-chain transactions. Network congestion, validator issues, or chain-level incidents on BNB Smart Chain may affect transaction processing and platform availability.

## 7. Liquidity risk

INKY maintains liquidity on PancakeSwap v2 in the INKY/USDT pair. Liquidity depth is variable and depends on liquidity providers. Large trades relative to pool depth may experience slippage. There is no guarantee that liquidity will be maintained at any specific level.

Changes in applicable law may restrict platform access, require operational changes, or affect the classification and value of digital assets. Users are responsible for understanding and complying with all tax obligations, reporting requirements, and legal restrictions applicable in their jurisdiction. Inkryptus does not provide tax or legal advice and does not withhold taxes on behalf of users.
