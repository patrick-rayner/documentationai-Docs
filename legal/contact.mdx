## Legal and compliance inquiries

For formal, legal, or compliance-related communications, contact the Inkryptus team via email:

**support@inkryptus.com**

All communications are internally recorded and handled according to the priority levels defined in the [Terms of Use](/legal/terms).

<Callout kind="alert">
  Inkryptus will never request passwords, private keys, seed phrases, or authentication codes through any channel, including official support. Any such request should be treated as a fraud attempt.
</Callout>
