## Support

The primary source of information and assistance for any questions or issues is the Inkryptus support team, available through two channels:

<Columns cols="2">
  <Card title="Email Support" icon="mail">
    Send a message to **support@inkryptus.com** for technical, operational, or account-related requests. All communications are internally recorded and handled according to the priority levels defined in the Terms of Use.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In-App Ticket" icon="message-circle">
    Open the Inkryptus app, navigate to **Support > Open New Ticket**, and describe your request. Tickets are tracked and assigned to the appropriate team.
  </Card>
</Columns>

### Chat with Inkryptus AI

For faster answers, use the AI-powered chat available in the app under **Support > Chat with Support**. The assistant can help with common questions, guide you through platform features, and troubleshoot issues in real time.

If the AI is unable to resolve your request, a support ticket will be automatically opened with the full conversation context, ensuring continuity and no need to repeat information.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Inkryptus will never request passwords, private keys, seed phrases, or authentication codes via email, social media, or any channel other than the official app and the @inkryptus.com email domain. Any such request should be treated as a fraud attempt.
</Callout>
