## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A smartphone with iOS 15+ or Android 8.0+
- Stable internet connection
- A small amount of cryptocurrency (e.g., ETH or BTC) in an external wallet for funding
- Backup method ready (e.g., secure notes app for recovery phrase)
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your secure Inkryptus wallet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch the App" icon="smartphone">
    Open Inkryptus and tap **Create New Wallet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Secure Your Wallet" icon="lock">
    Write down your 12-word recovery phrase securely. Never share it.

    <Callout kind="danger">
      Losing your recovery phrase means permanent loss of funds. Store it offline.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify and Complete" icon="check-circle">
    Confirm the phrase by re-entering it. Set a strong PIN or enable biometrics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fund Your Wallet

Transfer cryptocurrency from an external wallet or exchange to Inkryptus.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Deposit Address" icon="copy">
    In the app, go to **Wallet** > **Receive**. Copy the address for your chosen network (e.g., Ethereum).

    ```
    0x742d35Cc6bA9A0780D4aD8e6eD5e5fB8aC123456
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer Funds" icon="arrow-up-circle">
    From your external wallet, send a small test amount first (e.g., 0.01 ETH).

    <Image
      src="/images/wallet-deposit.png"
      alt="Wallet deposit screen"
      width="300"
      height="600"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Receipt" icon="check">
    Wait 1-5 minutes for confirmation. Check balance in **Wallet** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make Your First Transaction

Send crypto to another wallet or start swapping INKY tokens.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Send" icon="send">
    Tap **Send**, paste recipient address, enter amount (e.g., 0.005 ETH).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Confirm" icon="eye">
    Verify details, adjust gas fees if needed, then **Confirm** with PIN/biometrics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Transaction" icon="activity">
    View status in **Activity**. Transactions confirm in `<60` seconds on fast networks.

    Example tx hash:
    ```
    0x1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
