Inkryptus runs promotional campaigns to drive adoption, reward early users, and celebrate community milestones. Campaigns are distinct from fee-based commissions: they are funded from the Inkryptus marketing budget, have fixed rules, and run for a defined period.

<Callout kind="info">
  For partner-specific incentives (Staking Referral, formerly Fast Bonus), see [Partner Program: Staking Referral](/partner-program/staking-referral).
</Callout>

## Campaign Timeline

<Update label="Pizza Day 2026" description="Thu 21 May to Sun 24 May 2026, 00:00-23:59 UTC" tags={["Active", "Community"]}>
  **Eligible**: new user who, within the window, creates an Inkryptus account, passes KYC, has no prior staking contract, and opens a staking contract of 100+ INKY in a 12-month or longer pool.

  **Bonus**: promotional staking contract of 10 INKY, matching the same lock period and yield conditions as the user's main contract.

  **Limit**: 1 per account. No maximum cap on total distributed bonuses.

  **Funding**: Inkryptus marketing budget.

  The campaign targets first-time stakers during Bitcoin Pizza Day weekend. The bonus contract runs in parallel with the user's main contract under identical terms.
</Update>

<Update label="Lucky Flip 2025" description="22 May 2025 (single day)" tags={["Ended", "Community"]}>
  **Mechanic**: first CoinFlip of the day paid 2x the normal win amount (if the player won).

  **Eligible**: all active users.

  **Funding**: Inkryptus marketing budget.

  A single-day celebration of Bitcoin Pizza Day. Players who won their first CoinFlip of the day received double the standard payout.
</Update>

## How Campaigns Work

- Each campaign has its own eligibility criteria, bonus structure, and time window.
- Bonuses are credited automatically when conditions are met.
- Campaign details are announced via official channels (in-app notifications, email, social media, community groups).
- Active campaigns and their status are visible in the user dashboard when applicable.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Campaigns are not guaranteed income. Inkryptus may modify or terminate a campaign at any time based on budget or strategic decisions.
</Callout>
