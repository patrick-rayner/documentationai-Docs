Inkryptus runs promotional campaigns to drive adoption, reward early users, and celebrate community milestones. Campaigns are distinct from fee-based commissions: they are funded from Inkryptus marketing budget or vesting reserves, have fixed rules, and run for a defined period.

For the ongoing Staking Referral program (permanent until fund depletion), see [Staking Referral](/campaigns/staking-referral).

## Active Campaigns

### Pizza Day 2026 (MVP)

| Attribute | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Window** | Thu 21 May to Sun 24 May 2026, 00:00 to 23:59 UTC |
| **Eligible** | New user who, within the window: creates an Inkryptus account, passes KYC, has no prior staking contract, and opens a staking contract of 100+ INKY in a 12-month or longer pool |
| **Bonus** | Promotional staking contract of 10 INKY, matching the same lock period and yield conditions as the user's main contract |
| **Limit** | 1 per account. No maximum cap on total distributed bonuses |
| **Funding** | Inkryptus marketing budget |

<Callout kind="tip">
  The campaign targets first-time stakers during Bitcoin Pizza Day weekend. The bonus contract runs in parallel with the user's main contract under identical terms.
</Callout>

## How Campaigns Work

- Each campaign has its own eligibility criteria, bonus structure, and time window.
- Bonuses are credited automatically when conditions are met.
- Campaign details are announced via official channels (in-app notifications, email, social media, community groups).
- Active campaigns and their status are visible in the IBO or user dashboard when applicable.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Campaigns are not guaranteed income. Inkryptus may modify or terminate a campaign at any time based on budget or strategic decisions.
</Callout>
