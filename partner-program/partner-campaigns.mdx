Partner Campaigns are marketing incentives exclusive to the Partner Program. They are funded from Inkryptus reserves (not from fee-based commissions), have their own eligibility rules, and run independently of the standard commission model.

No campaigns are currently active. This page will be updated when new campaigns are announced.

## How Partner Campaigns differ from commissions

| Aspect | Partner Campaigns | Fee-Based Commissions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Funding** | Inkryptus reserves (marketing budget) | User operation fees (30/70 split) |
| **Duration** | Fixed window or until fund depletion | Continuous |
| **Scope** | Varies per campaign | Up to 7 levels |
| **Activation** | Automatic when conditions are met | Automatic on every qualifying operation |

For the standard commission model, see [Commissions](/partner-program/commissions).

## History

### Fast Bonus (discontinued at V2.8.0)

Rewarded IBOs (up to 7 levels) when users in their network opened staking contracts of 6+ months. Funded from Inkryptus vault. Bonus applied only on the excess above the user's previous highest stake (High Staked Amount watermark).

| Lock-up period | Total bonus | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 | Level 4 | Level 5 | Level 6 | Level 7 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **12 to 36 months** | 10% | 5.00% | 2.00% | 1.00% | 0.50% | 0.50% | 0.50% | 0.50% |
| **6 months** | 5% | 2.50% | 1.00% | 0.50% | 0.25% | 0.25% | 0.25% | 0.25% |
