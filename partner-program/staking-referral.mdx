This is a marketing campaign, not a fee-based commission. It is funded from a dedicated 1,000,000 INKY vesting reserve and pays Level 1 sponsors only.

## Program Overview

| Attribute          | Value                                             |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **Status**         | Active campaign; replaced Fast Bonus              |
| **Funding Source** | 1,000,000 INKY from vesting reserves              |
| **Counter Start**  | V2.8.0 release (zero at launch)                   |
| **Duration**       | Until fund depletion (auto-terminate)             |
| **Visibility**     | IBO Dashboard > Earnings card (real-time counter) |
| **Payment Scope**  | Level 1 only (direct sponsor); no higher levels   |
| **Rate**           | 5% of new stake amount                            |

## Bonus Calculation: High Staked Amount Rule

The bonus is **not paid on the full stake amount**. Instead, it applies only to the **excess above the user's High Staked Amount watermark**.

<Callout kind="info">
  **High Staked Amount** is the highest individual stake amount ever made by that user in any eligible contract. It only increases; it never resets.
</Callout>

### Example 1: First Stake (2,000 INKY in INKY Pool 12m)

| Level         | Rate | Previous High Staked | New Stake  | Calculation Base | Bonus Paid |
| ------------- | ---- | -------------------- | ---------- | ---------------- | ---------- |
| Level 1 (You) | 5%   | 0 INKY               | 2,000 INKY | 2,000 INKY       | 100 INKY   |

First stake ever: full amount is eligible because High Staked Amount was zero.

### Example 2: Re-Stake at Same Level (2,000 INKY, same user)

| Level         | Rate | Previous High Staked | New Stake  | Calculation Base | Bonus Paid |
| ------------- | ---- | -------------------- | ---------- | ---------------- | ---------- |
| Level 1 (You) | 5%   | 2,000 INKY           | 2,000 INKY | 0 INKY           | 0 INKY     |

No bonus: the new stake matches the previous high, so there is no incremental increase.

### Example 3: Larger Stake (3,000 INKY, same user)

| Level         | Rate | Previous High Staked | New Stake  | Calculation Base | Bonus Paid |
| ------------- | ---- | -------------------- | ---------- | ---------------- | ---------- |
| Level 1 (You) | 5%   | 2,000 INKY           | 3,000 INKY | 1,000 INKY       | 50 INKY    |

Bonus applies to the excess: only the 1,000 INKY difference above the previous record (3,000 - 2,000 = 1,000) is eligible. 1,000 x 5% = 50 INKY.

## Campaign Termination

The campaign automatically terminates when the 1,000,000 INKY fund is fully distributed. At that point:

- No new bonuses are paid, regardless of new stakes
- Counter remains visible in the dashboard showing "Fund Depleted"
- Staking operations continue normally (no operational impact)
- The campaign may be re-launched in the future with a new funding allocation

## Key Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Fund-limited** | Campaign ends when 1,000,000 INKY is exhausted |
| **No cost to affiliate** | Bonus is funded by Inkryptus, not deducted from user staking |
| **Level 1 only** | Uplines do not receive Staking Referral bonuses |
| **Watermark prevents double-dipping** | Same user cannot earn bonus on the same stake amount twice |
| **Automatic calculation** | Bonus is credited in real-time when eligible contract is activated |
| **Per-user tracking** | High Staked Amount is tracked per user, across all eligible contracts combined |

## Dashboard Visibility

The real-time counter in `IBO > Earnings` card shows: total INKY paid out so far, remaining INKY available in the fund, date of last bonus credited, and average bonus per eligible referral.
