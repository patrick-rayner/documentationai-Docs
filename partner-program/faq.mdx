---

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What is an IBO?" default-open="true">
    An **IBO (Independent Business Owner)** is a participant in the Inkryptus Partner Program. IBOs promote the platform, refer new users, and earn commissions based on real activity generated within their network (up to 7 levels deep). Commissions come from a share of platform fees, not from user recruitment fees or product purchases.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How does the commission model work?" default-open="false">
    The Partner Program shares platform fees with IBOs based on real activity:

    - **Activity-based commissions:** Earnings come from fees charged on actual platform usage (staking, swaps, harvests, games).
    - **No inventory requirement:** IBOs do not purchase kits, bundles, or inventory.
    - **No mandatory purchases:** Users can register on the platform and use it without becoming an IBO.
    - **Utility-driven:** Users earn staking rewards and access products regardless of IBO status.

    Commissions are credited daily in INKY based on network activity.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What is the network size limit?" default-open="false">
    There is no limit to network size. IBOs can build networks of unlimited depth in user count. However, **commissions are distributed only up to the 7th level** of network depth (10% per level). Referrals beyond level 7 do not generate commissions for the sponsoring IBO.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What happens if my subscription lapses?" default-open="false">
    When an IBO's subscription becomes inactive (payment not renewed):

    - The IBO status changes to **inactive**.
    - **No new users** can be added to the network.
    - **No new commissions** are earned while inactive.
    - **Existing network members remain linked** and their activity is preserved; the network is not deleted.
    - **Reactivation:** The IBO can resume subscription at any time and begin receiving commissions again. However, commissions earned during the inactive period are lost; they cannot be retroactively claimed.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What currency are commissions paid in?" default-open="false">
    All Partner Program commissions are paid in **INKY**.

    Other earnings: staking rewards are paid in the same currency as the staked contract (USDT, INKY, CAKE, etc.), and game prizes are paid in INKY.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I verify my commission payments?" default-open="false">
    Commission payments are transparent and verifiable through two methods:

    **1. In-App Dashboard**: Access `IBO > Earnings` to view historical commission accruals and real-time payment status. The dashboard updates daily at 00:30 UTC.

    **2. On-Chain Verification**: All Partner Program commissions are recorded in public smart contracts on BNB Smart Chain. Use [BscScan](https://bscscan.com) to inspect contract address, transaction history for your wallet address, and token transfers.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="30-Day Sponsor Window" default-open="false">
    Users who register without a sponsor have 30 days to add one manually:

    - **Path:** `Profile > Account > Sponsor`
    - **Deadline:** Strictly 30 days from account creation.
    - **After 30 days:** No sponsor can be added.

    This window is non-negotiable and cannot be extended.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are commissions retroactive?" default-open="false">
    No. Commissions are only credited after the sponsor linkage is established.

    If a user registers without a sponsor and the sponsor is added later (within the 30-day window), commissions generated during the unsponsored period are **forfeited to Inkryptus**. Only commissions earned *after* the sponsor is linked are credited to the IBO.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What happened to the Fast Bonus?" default-open="false">
    Fast Bonus was discontinued at V2.8.0. New incentive campaigns may be introduced in the future.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What are my tax obligations?" default-open="false">
    Tax responsibility rests solely with the IBO:

    - **Income Reporting:** All Partner Program earnings must be reported to local tax authorities.
    - **No Withholding:** Inkryptus does not withhold taxes or submit filings on behalf of IBOs.
    - **Documentation:** Request an Account Report via `Profile > Account > Statement` for a record of earnings.
    - **Professional Advice:** Consult a tax professional in your jurisdiction.

    Failure to comply with tax obligations is the sole responsibility of the IBO.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
