## What is an IBO?

An **IBO (Independent Business Owner)** is a participant in the Inkryptus Partner Program. IBOs promote the platform, refer new users, and earn commissions based on real activity generated within their network (up to 7 levels deep). Commissions come from a share of platform fees, not from user recruitment fees or product purchases.

## How does the commission model work?

The Partner Program shares platform fees with IBOs based on real activity:

- **Activity-based commissions:** Earnings come from fees charged on actual platform usage (staking, swaps, harvests, games).
- **No inventory requirement:** IBOs do not purchase kits, bundles, or inventory.
- **No mandatory purchases:** Users can register on the platform and use it without becoming an IBO.
- **Utility-driven:** Users earn staking rewards and access products regardless of IBO status.

Commissions are credited daily in INKY based on network activity.

## What is the network size limit?

There is no limit to network size. IBOs can build networks of unlimited depth in user count.

However, **commissions are distributed only up to the 7th level** of network depth (10% per level). Referrals beyond level 7 do not generate commissions for the sponsoring IBO.

## What happens if my subscription lapses?

When an IBO's subscription becomes inactive (payment not renewed):

- The IBO status changes to **inactive**.
- **No new users** can be added to the network.
- **No new commissions** are earned while inactive.
- **Existing network members remain linked** and their activity is preserved; the network is not deleted.
- **Reactivation:** The IBO can resume subscription at any time and begin receiving commissions again. However, commissions earned during the inactive period are lost; they cannot be retroactively claimed.

## What currency are commissions paid in?

All Partner Program commissions are paid in **INKY**.

Other earnings:

- **Staking rewards:** Paid in the same currency as the staked contract (USDT, INKY, CAKE, etc.).
- **Staking Referral bonus:** Paid in INKY.
- **Game prizes:** Paid in INKY.

## How do I verify my commission payments?

Commission payments are transparent and verifiable through two methods:

### 1. In-App Dashboard

- Access `IBO > Earnings` to view historical commission accruals and real-time payment status.
- The dashboard updates daily at 00:30 UTC.

### 2. On-Chain Verification

- All Partner Program commissions are recorded in public smart contracts on BNB Smart Chain.
- Use **BscScan** (<https://bscscan.com>) to inspect:
  - Contract address (available in the app and documentation)
  - Transaction history for your wallet address
  - Token transfers and amounts

This dual verification ensures transparency and prevents disputes.

## 30-Day Sponsor Window

Users who register without a sponsor have 30 days to add one manually:

- **Path:** `Profile > Account > Sponsor`
- **Deadline:** Strictly 30 days from account creation.
- **After 30 days:** No sponsor can be added.

This window is non-negotiable and cannot be extended. Plan referrals accordingly.

## Are commissions retroactive?

No. Commissions are only credited after the sponsor linkage is established.

**Important:** If a user registers without a sponsor and the sponsor is added later (within the 30-day window), commissions generated during the unsponsored period are **forfeited to Inkryptus**. Only commissions earned *after* the sponsor is linked are credited to the IBO.

## Am I eligible for Staking Referral?

Staking Referral is a separate marketing campaign with specific eligibility rules:

### Eligible Contracts

- **INKY Pool 12-month staking**
- **INKY Pool 36-month staking**

### Ineligible Contracts

- INKY Pool Free/Flexible
- INKY Pool 6 months (discontinued)
- INKY Pool 24 months (discontinued)
- USDT Pool
- CAKE Pool
- Any other staking contract

### Who Receives the Bonus

- **IBO Level 1 only** (the direct sponsor of the referred user).
- The bonus is **not distributed** across deeper levels (unlike regular commissions).

### Bonus Calculation

- **5% of the staking amount** in INKY for eligible contracts.
- **High Staked Amount rule:** The bonus is paid only on the amount that exceeds the user's previous all-time high stake. Subsequent stakes equal to or below that high mark receive no bonus.

### Bonus Payout

- **Immediate** upon contract activation (not daily like regular commissions).

For complete details, see the Staking Referral section in the main Partner Program documentation.

## What are my tax obligations?

Tax responsibility rests solely with the IBO:

1. **Income Reporting:** All Partner Program earnings must be reported to local tax authorities in accordance with local tax law.

2. **Tax Calculation:** The IBO is responsible for calculating applicable taxes, which may include income tax, capital gains tax, or other levies depending on jurisdiction.

3. **No Withholding:** Inkryptus **does not withhold taxes** or submit filings to any authority on behalf of IBOs.

4. **Documentation:** IBOs may request an Account Report via `Profile > Account > Statement` to obtain a record of earnings suitable for tax preparation.

5. **Professional Advice:** Consult a tax professional in your jurisdiction to determine your exact obligations. Rules vary significantly by country and individual circumstance.

Failure to comply with tax obligations is the sole responsibility of the IBO and may result in penalties from local authorities.
