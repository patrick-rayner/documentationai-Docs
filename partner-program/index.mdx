The Partner Program is Inkryptus' official revenue-sharing model for independent partners. Partners, called IBOs (Independent Business Owners), promote Inkryptus products and earn commissions from real platform activity generated by their networks.

## Overview

The Partner Program operates on a single core principle: commissions derive from actual usage fees, not recruitment. When network members use staking, buy/sell, harvest, games, or other products, Inkryptus shares 70% of the applicable fees with the partner network in proportion to each level's activity.

Unlike traditional referral schemes, the Partner Program:

- **Requires real activity** — commissions only flow from genuine transactions and staking activity
- **Shares fees transparently** — 30% retained by Inkryptus for operations; 70% distributed across up to 7 network levels
- **Is verifiable** — all distributions recorded on public smart contracts on BNB Smart Chain
- **Enforces ethical communication** — partners must avoid misleading claims, guaranteed returns, or hidden risk

## Who can join

The Partner Program is open to:

- Content creators and influencers in crypto, finance, or technology
- Investors already using Inkryptus who want to monetize their network
- Communities, study groups, educators, and networking hubs seeking a platform for passive income in crypto

## Core mechanics

1. Create an Inkryptus account and activate the Partner Program in the app
2. Receive a unique referral link; users who sign up via this link join your Level 1
3. Set up automatic daily commission credits as your network uses the platform
4. Monitor network activity, earnings, and bonuses in the Partner dashboard
5. Pay the US$24/month subscription (after 30 free days) to stay active

When your active subscription lapses, new users no longer join your network and commission payouts pause. Reactivate anytime by resuming payments.

## Subscription and cost

- First 30 days: free trial
- After trial: US$24/month, paid in **USDT** via the IBO dashboard in the app
- Renewal is **manual** each month (automatic renewal planned for a future release)
- No mandatory purchases; zero initial investment required
- USDT was chosen to simplify access for new partners who may not yet hold INKY
- Subscription unlocks: advanced dashboard, full network visibility, all commission types, bonus eligibility

## Key principles

**Real Activity** — No income from passive signups. Commissions originate from staking rewards, transaction fees, performance fees, and gaming winnings generated by network members.

**Merit-Based** — Earnings scale with network growth and platform usage. Larger, more active networks generate higher commissions.

**Transparency** — All distributions follow fixed formulas (30/70 split) and settle daily. Every transaction is publicly verifiable on BNB Smart Chain.

**Ethical** — Partners must communicate truthfully, disclose risks, avoid guaranteed return claims, and rely only on verifiable data.

## Payment and settlement

Partner commissions settle daily in INKY. Staking rewards come in the contract's native currency (USDT, INKY, CAKE). Gaming winnings pay in INKY. The payment calendar details all settlement frequencies and terms.

Compliance note: Partners are responsible for tax obligations in their jurisdiction. Inkryptus does not withhold taxes.
