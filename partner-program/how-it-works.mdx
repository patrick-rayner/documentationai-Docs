## Onboarding

To become an IBO (Independent Business Owner):

1. Download the Inkryptus app and create your account
2. Navigate to **IBO Program** section
3. Accept the terms and conditions for Independent Business Owner status
4. Your account is immediately activated with a 30-day free trial of the Partner dashboard
5. After 30 days, a US$24/month subscription is required to maintain active status

While your subscription is active, you can receive new users in your network and earn all commission types. If subscription lapses, your account enters inactive status: no new signups join your network, and commission payouts stop. You may reactivate anytime.

## Referral link and network structure

Each active IBO receives a unique referral link. Users who sign up via this link become your **Level 1** direct referrals. When Level 1 users invite others, those new users form your **Level 2**, and the pattern continues through **Level 7**.

The network depth is unlimited, but commission distributions cap at 7 levels. Unused commissions (levels 8+) are retained by Inkryptus.

If a user signs up without a referral link, they have a 30-day window from account creation to manually assign a sponsor via **Profile > Account > Sponsor**. After 30 days, sponsor assignment is locked.

## Commission mechanics

Commissions are calculated daily based on real platform activity: staking contracts, buy/sell transactions, harvest operations, performance fees, and gaming winnings.

The distribution model is uniform across all commission types:

- **30%** of collected fees stays with Inkryptus (operations, security, infrastructure, development)
- **70%** of collected fees distributes across levels of the partner network: **10% per level**, up to 7 levels

When a network level does not exist (no upline sponsor), that level's 10% allocation returns to Inkryptus and funds community campaigns.

For detailed commission tables and examples, see [Commissions](./commissions.mdx).

## Dashboard sections

The Partner Program dashboard provides four main sections:

### Subscription

Displays subscription status, renewal date, and payment method. Here you can manage billing and view upcoming charges. The first 30 days are free; after that, US$24 is charged monthly.

### Earnings

Shows real-time and historical commission totals broken down by type:

- Subscription commissions (from monthly IBO fees)
- Transaction commissions (from Withdraw, Swap, Harvest operations)
- Performance commissions (from staking cycle profits)
- Gaming commissions (from CoinFlip and other games)
- **Staking Referral balance** — displays remaining fund balance for the Staking Referral campaign and historical payouts

All earnings are credited daily in INKY. Daily settlements occur at 00:30 UTC.

### Network

Displays your referral tree in real-time. Shows each level's member count, active users, and total network activity. Network events (new signups, user activity) update instantly.

### Wallet

Separate IBO Wallet for earnings and bonuses. Distinct from user wallet. All commissions, Staking Referral bonuses, and special promotion payouts land in the IBO Wallet. You may withdraw to bank account or transfer funds onward at any time, subject to withdrawal limits and KYC tier.

## IBO Wallet vs User Wallet

IBO earnings are isolated in a dedicated Wallet within your IBO Program account. This separation ensures:

- Clear accounting of partner earnings vs personal holdings
- Transparent commission tracking
- Simplified tax reporting and auditing
- Distinct withdrawal limits and compliance rules

Your User Wallet (staking account, buy/sell activity, gaming rewards) and IBO Wallet are fully separate. Earnings from each settle independently.

## Payment frequency

All Partner Program commissions settle **daily** (00:30 UTC). Staking Referral bonuses settle **immediately** upon eligible contract activation. See the payment calendar in [Commissions](./commissions.mdx) for complete settlement terms.
