<Callout kind="alert">
  Fast Bonus was discontinued at V2.8.0 and replaced by [Staking Referral](/partner-program/staking-referral). This page is kept for historical reference.
</Callout>

## Overview

Fast Bonus was a partner incentive that rewarded IBOs when users in their network (up to 7 levels deep) opened new staking contracts of 6 months or longer. The bonus was funded from the Inkryptus vault, keeping the user's staked amount intact.

## Key rules

- Bonus applied only to contracts of 6 months or longer.
- The bonus was paid only on the **excess above the user's previous highest stake** (same watermark concept as Staking Referral).
- The bonus was not cumulative: to receive it again from the same user, that user had to exceed their previous record.
- Funded by INKY from the Inkryptus vault.

## Bonus rates

| Lock-up period | Total bonus | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 | Level 4 | Level 5 | Level 6 | Level 7 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **12 to 36 months** | 10% | 5.00% | 2.00% | 1.00% | 0.50% | 0.50% | 0.50% | 0.50% |
| **6 months** | 5% | 2.50% | 1.00% | 0.50% | 0.25% | 0.25% | 0.25% | 0.25% |

## Differences from Staking Referral

| Aspect | Fast Bonus | Staking Referral |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Levels** | Up to 7 | Level 1 only |
| **Eligible contracts** | 6, 12, 24, 36 months | 12, 36 months only |
| **Rate** | 5% or 10% (split across levels) | 5% (100% to Level 1) |
| **Fund** | Inkryptus vault (no fixed cap) | 1,000,000 INKY dedicated fund |
| **Status** | Discontinued (V2.8.0) | Active |

Staking Referral is a new program, not a rename of Fast Bonus.
