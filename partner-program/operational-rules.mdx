These rules define how the Partner Program operates. All IBOs must comply with these requirements to maintain active status and receive commissions.

## 30-Day Sponsor Window

Users who register without an IBO sponsor have exactly 30 days (from account creation) to manually add one.

- **Path:** `Profile > Account > Sponsor`
- **After 30 days:** No sponsor can be added; the account remains unlinked.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Commissions generated before sponsor linkage are **not retroactive**. They remain with Inkryptus and are not credited to the IBO.
</Callout>

This window provides flexibility for users to identify and link to an IBO after account creation while preventing indefinite retroactive claims.

## Subscription and Payment

### Pricing

| Period | Cost |
|--------|------|
| First 30 days | Free trial with full dashboard access |
| After trial | US$24/month to maintain active IBO status |

### Payment method

The subscription is paid in **USDT**, directly from the IBO dashboard in the app. USDT was chosen to simplify access for new partners who may not yet hold INKY. No other payment methods are currently accepted.

### Renewal

<Callout kind="info">
  Renewal is **manual**. The IBO must actively renew each month through the dashboard. There is no automatic recurring charge. Automatic renewal is planned for a future release.
</Callout>

### Active subscription requirement

An IBO's subscription must be active for new users to be added to their network.

- New user registration via an IBO's link is only effective if the IBO has an **active, paid subscription** at the moment of registration.
- IBOs with inactive subscriptions do not receive new network members.
- Reactivating a subscription does not retroactively credit previously registered users during the inactive period.

## Contact Sharing (Opt-in)

The **username** of the sponsor is always shared with referred users and cannot be disabled.

Additional contact data is opt-in and disabled by default:

| Field | Default | Path |
|-------|---------|------|
| Name | Opt-in (off) | `Profile > Account > Sponsor` |
| Email | Opt-in (off) | `Profile > Account > Sponsor` |
| Phone | Opt-in (off) | `Profile > Account > Sponsor` |

IBOs can enable any combination or keep all contact data private. Users referred can see which contact fields the sponsor has enabled.

## Real-Time Network Timeline

The Partner Program dashboard displays network activity in two modes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Network Timeline" icon="activity">
    **Real-time updates**

    Sections like `IBO > Network` show new user registrations in **real time**. This reflects live signup activity without delay.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Earnings Updates" icon="clock">
    **Daily at 00:30 UTC**

    The `IBO > Earnings` section updates once daily and covers: commission accruals, campaign bonuses, and historical earnings summary.

    Users may see new network members appear instantly but should expect a 24-hour delay before earnings reflect from those members' activity.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## IBO Wallet Structure

### Overview

- **IBO Wallet:** Holds all commissions and bonuses earned through the Partner Program.
- **User Wallet:** Holds personal assets (USDT, INKY, BTCB, etc.).
- These wallets are distinct and require explicit transfer actions.

### Sub-wallets

| Sub-wallet | Receives | Transfer to User Wallet |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Harvest** | Performance fee commissions | US$3 fee per operation |
| **Transfer** | All other commissions (subscription, transaction, Arena) | No fee, immediate |

### Moving funds to User Wallet

- **From Harvest sub-wallet:** US$3 fee per harvest operation. Fees are deducted from the IBO Wallet balance.
- **From Transfer sub-wallet:** No fee. Immediate.
- **Purpose:** Move IBO earnings to the user wallet to spend, stake, or withdraw.

<Callout kind="tip">
  This structure clarifies the origin of funds, protects against accidental spending of earned commissions, and allows IBOs to track earnings independently from personal assets.
</Callout>
