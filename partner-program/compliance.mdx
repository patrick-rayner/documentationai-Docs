Inkryptus operates the Partner Program with strict compliance and anti-abuse safeguards to ensure a fair, transparent, and sustainable ecosystem for all participants.

## Anti-Abuse Rules

### Multi-Account Prohibition

Creating multiple accounts to self-refer, artificially inflate network size, or bypass program rules is strictly prohibited.

<Callout kind="danger">
  Violation of this rule may result in account suspension, permanent banishment, or commission forfeiture. Inkryptus reserves the right to identify and act on suspicious account patterns.
</Callout>

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

KYC verification was introduced in app version 2.0 (2026) to establish legitimacy and ensure compliance with applicable regulations. All IBOs are required to complete KYC verification to maintain active status. KYC data is handled confidentially and used solely for identity confirmation and fraud prevention.

### Ethical Communication Standards

Marketing and promotional activities must adhere to strict ethical guidelines.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Prohibited" icon="x-circle">
    - Promising guaranteed returns or risk-free gains
    - Concealing or downplaying cryptocurrency volatility and investment risks
    - Using misleading presentations, false statistics, or exaggerated performance claims
    - Misrepresenting the nature or mechanics of the program
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Required" icon="check-circle">
    - All claims must be grounded in public and verifiable data accessible via the app, BscScan, or published documentation
    - Clear communication that earnings depend on real platform usage, not passive recruitment
    - Transparent disclosure of risks inherent to cryptocurrency and blockchain-based systems
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Ongoing Monitoring

Inkryptus continuously monitors Partner Program activity for suspicious patterns. Accounts engaging in fraud, market manipulation, or systematic rule-breaking are subject to investigation. Consequences may include temporary suspension, permanent banishment, or legal action where applicable.

## Fiscal Obligations

### Income Attribution

All commissions earned through the Partner Program are credited directly to the IBO's wallet within the Inkryptus platform (IBO Wallet).

### Tax Responsibility

<Callout kind="alert">
  Inkryptus **does not withhold taxes** or submit tax filings on behalf of IBOs. Each participant must independently determine and fulfill their local fiscal requirements.
</Callout>

The IBO is solely responsible for complying with tax and fiscal obligations in their jurisdiction, including reporting income to local tax authorities, filing required tax returns, and paying applicable taxes, duties, and levies.

### Tax Documentation

IBOs may request an Account Report via `Profile > Account > Statement` to obtain a detailed record of all earnings and transactions. This report can assist in tax preparation and documentation.

## Prohibited Claims

### No Guarantee Language

- Never state or imply that returns are guaranteed, assured, or risk-free.
- Do not use phrases like "guaranteed income," "sure profits," or "zero risk."

### No Misleading Marketing

- All promotional content must be factually accurate and based on verifiable data.
- Do not exaggerate or misrepresent historical performance as predictive of future results.
- Avoid selective presentation of positive outcomes without disclosing full risk context.

### Data Verification Requirement

Any claim about program mechanics, historical performance, or earning potential must be:

<Steps>
  <Step title="App verification" icon="smartphone" title-type="p">
    Publicly verifiable via the Inkryptus app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="On-chain records" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Documented in BscScan smart contract records.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Official documentation" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Referenced in this documentation or official help center materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Claims not meeting these criteria must not be used in promotional materials.

## Enforcement and Violations

Violations of these rules may result in:

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Minor | Warning notice |
| Moderate | Temporary suspension of commission payouts |
| Severe | Permanent account suspension |
| Critical | Forfeiture of earned commissions |
| Legal | Referral to law enforcement or regulatory authorities (if applicable) |

Inkryptus reserves the right to update these rules and enforce them retroactively for violations discovered after the update date.
