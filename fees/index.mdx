## Overview

Fees are platform charges levied on user operations. They fund infrastructure, development, liquidity, and the Partner Program commission pool.

For details on how fees are distributed to the partner network, see [Commissions](/partner-program/commissions).

## Fee Schedule

| Category             | Applies To                          | Amount              |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| **Performance fee**  | Net profit from staking/farming     | 25% of daily profit |
| **Withdrawal fee**   | Withdrawing funds from the platform | US$3/operation      |
| **Swap fee**         | Exchanging between assets           | US$3/operation      |
| **Harvest fee**      | Collecting accumulated rewards      | US$3/operation      |
| **Partner Subscription** | Partner Program access              | US$24/month         |

## Withdrawal Limits

Withdrawal capacity is tiered by KYC verification status:

| Tier       | KYC Status | Monthly Limit | Reset                      |
| ---------- | ---------- | ------------- | -------------------------- |
| **Bronze** | Inactive   | US$500        | 1st of month, 00:00:01 UTC |
| **Silver** | Active     | US$500,000    | 1st of month, 00:00:01 UTC |

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  To upgrade from Bronze to Silver, complete KYC in `Profile > Account > KYC`. Current limits are visible in `Profile > Account > Fees`.
</Callout>
