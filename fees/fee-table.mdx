## Fee Schedule

| Fee Category | Applies To | Amount | Charged When |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Performance fee** | Net profit from staking/farming | 25% of daily profit | Daily, on yield generated. Only charged if profit > 0. |
| **Withdrawal fee** | Withdrawing funds from the platform | US$3/operation | At time of execution |
| **Swap fee** | Exchanging between assets | US$3/operation | At time of execution |
| **Harvest fee** | Collecting accumulated staking/farming rewards | US$3/operation | At time of execution |
| **Partner Subscription** | Partner Program access (dashboard, network, commissions, tools) | US$24/month | After 30-day trial. Non-payment suspends access and commission eligibility. |

## Withdrawal Limits

Monthly withdrawal capacity by KYC tier:

| Tier | Verification | Monthly Limit | Reset Schedule |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Bronze** | No KYC | US$500/month | 1st of month, 00:00:01 UTC |
| **Silver** | KYC Active | US$500,000/month | 1st of month, 00:00:01 UTC |

<Callout kind="tip">
  **Upgrading tiers:** Access KYC form in `Profile > Account > KYC`. View current limit status in `Profile > Account > Fees`.
</Callout>

<Callout kind="info">
  **Reset behavior:** Counter resets automatically on the 1st day of each month at 00:00:01 UTC. Withdrawals are cumulative within the monthly window. Exceeding the limit blocks further withdrawals until the reset date.
</Callout>
