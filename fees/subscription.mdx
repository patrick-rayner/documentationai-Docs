The Partner Program subscription provides access to the partner dashboard, network management tools, and commission eligibility.

## Pricing

| Attribute | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Amount** | US$24/month |
| **Payment method** | USDT, via Partner Program dashboard in the app |
| **Trial** | First 30 days free with full access |
| **Renewal** | Manual (automatic renewal planned for future release) |

## What the subscription includes

- Access to the Partner Program dashboard
- Full network visualization and activity tracking
- Eligibility to receive all commission types
- Access to Staking Referral and campaign bonuses

## Key rules

<Callout kind="alert">
  Non-payment suspends partner status and all commission payouts. New referrals are only linked to a partner with an active subscription at the time of registration. Reactivating a subscription does not retroactively credit users who registered during the inactive period.
</Callout>

USDT was chosen to simplify access for new partners who may not yet hold INKY.

For details on how subscription fees are distributed to the network, see [Commissions](/partner-program/commissions).
