Beyond the INKY Pool, Inkryptus offers staking on external ecosystem assets. These products operate exclusively in Flexible format (no lock-up) and pay rewards in the same asset deposited.

## USDT Stablecoin Farm

| Field | Value |
| ----- | ----- |
| Asset | USDT (Tether, BEP-20) |
| Format | Flexible (no lock-up) |
| Rewards | Daily, paid in USDT |
| Performance fee | 25% on profit |
| Harvest fee | US$3 per operation |
| Managed by | Products Vault ([`0xa307cf9e...`](https://bscscan.com/address/0xa307cf9e07692e7a31d0b42b970c180f3a2296ee)) |

USDT staking targets users who want to generate rewards with lower exposure to token price volatility, since USDT is pegged to the US dollar.

## CAKE Smart Farm

| Field | Value |
| ----- | ----- |
| Asset | CAKE (PancakeSwap, BEP-20) |
| Format | Flexible (no lock-up) |
| Rewards | Daily, paid in CAKE |
| Performance fee | 25% on profit |
| Harvest fee | US$3 per operation |
| Managed by | Products Vault ([`0xa307cf9e...`](https://bscscan.com/address/0xa307cf9e07692e7a31d0b42b970c180f3a2296ee)) |

CAKE is the native token of PancakeSwap, the largest DEX on BNB Smart Chain. The Inkryptus platform abstracts the DeFi interaction, allowing users to stake CAKE without needing to interact with PancakeSwap directly.

## Discontinued: BSW (Biswap)

<Callout kind="info">
  BSW staking is no longer available. It was previously offered in Flexible format with daily rewards paid in BSW.
</Callout>

## Reward mechanics

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="USDT Staking" icon="dollar-sign">
    USDT staking rewards are funded by the same INKY token emission that powers the INKY Pool. When users lock USDT, they inject liquidity into the platform, strengthening the INKY/USDT pool depth. In return, a portion of the daily INKY emission is allocated to USDT stakers and converted to USDT-denominated rewards.

    In short: locking USDT contributes to the platform's liquidity infrastructure, and the platform compensates that contribution through its emission model.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CAKE Staking" icon="layers">
    CAKE staking is an **encapsulated protocol from PancakeSwap**, similar to how Lido wraps ETH staking. The Inkryptus platform abstracts the interaction with PancakeSwap's native staking contracts, allowing users to stake CAKE without leaving the app. Rewards are generated by PancakeSwap's own reward mechanics and passed through to the user.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Common rules

All products share the same performance fee (25% on profit) and harvest fee (US$3 flat). Rewards do not auto-compound. Users must manually harvest and reinvest if they want to compound. See [Harvest flow](/features/staking/harvest) for the collection process and [Fees](/legal/fees) for the full fee schedule.
