Harvest is the process of collecting accumulated staking rewards from an active contract. Only the profit is transferred to the user's free wallet balance. The staked principal remains in the contract.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open staking dashboard" icon="bar-chart-2" title-type="p">
    User opens the staking dashboard in the Inkryptus app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View accumulated profit" icon="trending-up" title-type="p">
    Accumulated profit is displayed for each active contract.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Harvest" icon="download" title-type="p">
    User taps "Harvest" on the desired contract.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm fee" icon="credit-card" title-type="p">
    The fee (US$3 flat) is displayed before confirmation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profit collected" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Upon confirmation, the profit is moved to the free balance in the user's Inkryptus wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Rules

| Rule | Detail |
| ---- | ------ |
| Availability | 24 hours after contract activation |
| Fee | US$3 flat per operation |
| What moves | Profit only (principal stays invested) |
| Processing | Instant for harvests up to US$500 equivalent; above US$500 enters approval queue (up to 24h) |
| Frequency | Can be performed as often as desired after the initial 24h |
| Compound option | User can manually reinvest harvested profit into new contracts |

<Callout kind="info">
  The US$500 threshold is based on the USD equivalent value at the time of the harvest, regardless of the staked asset. For example, harvesting INKY profit worth US$600 at current price will enter the approval queue even though the payout is in INKY.
</Callout>

## What users can do with harvested profit

<Columns cols="2">
  <Card title="Reinvest (compound)" icon="refresh-cw" href="/features/staking/index" horizontal={true}>

    Swap into other assets (INKY, USDT, BTC, ETH, CAKE).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Withdraw" icon="upload" href="/features/wallet" horizontal={true}>
    Transfer to an external wallet (standard withdrawal fee applies).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use in ecosystem" icon="target" href="/features/arena" horizontal={true}>
    Use in Arena games, swaps, or other platform products.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Fee context

<Callout kind="info">
  The US$3 Harvest fee is the same flat fee applied to Swap and Withdraw operations. It covers the on-chain gas cost and platform processing. The fee is always displayed in the app before the user confirms the operation.
</Callout>

See [Fees](/introduction/fees) for the complete fee schedule.
