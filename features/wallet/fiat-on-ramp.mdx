Buy USDT with local currency (fiat) directly inside the Inkryptus app. The purchase flow is handled by a third-party payment provider integrated into the app.

<Callout kind="info">
  KYC verification is required for fiat purchases. GPS access may be requested by the payment provider to determine available payment methods for your region.
</Callout>

## Current provider

| Attribute | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Provider** | Simplex |
| **Asset purchased** | USDT (BEP-20) |
| **Minimum purchase** | US$50 |
| **Fee** | 3.5% (minimum US$5) + US$0.33 blockchain fee |
| **Payment methods** | Credit/debit card, other methods depending on region |
| **KYC** | Required |
| **GPS** | May be requested to detect local payment options |

### Fee examples

| Purchase amount | Provider fee (3.5%) | Blockchain fee | Total fee | USDT received (approx.) |

| US$50 | US$5.00 (minimum) | US$0.33 | US$5.33 | ~US$44.67 |
| US$100 | US$5.00 (minimum) | US$0.33 | US$5.33 | ~US$94.67 |
| US$500 | US$17.50 | US$0.33 | US$17.83 | ~US$482.17 |
| US$1,000 | US$35.00 | US$0.33 | US$35.33 | ~US$964.67 |

<Callout kind="alert">
  Fees are charged by the payment provider, not by Inkryptus. The exact amount received may vary slightly depending on exchange rates at the time of purchase.
</Callout>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Add Funds" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    On the Home screen, tap Add Funds and select Buy USDT.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose provider" icon="list" title-type="p">
    Select your preferred provider from the list. Currently, Simplex is available. More providers are planned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter the amount you wish to purchase (minimum US$50) and tap Confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to payment" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
    Tap Continue to payment. Allow GPS access if prompted (required by the payment provider to determine available payment methods).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete purchase" icon="credit-card" title-type="p">
    Inside the provider's screen, insert your payment method (card or another available option). Enter all required details: card number, cardholder name, billing address. Confirm the transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="USDT credited" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Once the provider confirms the payment, USDT is deposited automatically into your Inkryptus wallet. Your app balance updates accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
