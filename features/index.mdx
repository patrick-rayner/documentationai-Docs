### Wallet

The Inkryptus custodial wallet holds all user assets on the blockchain. Users deposit and withdraw crypto through the platform without managing private keys. Supports BTC, ETH, BNB, USDT, INKY, CAKE, and additional assets. See [Wallet](/features/wallet).

### Staking

Users stake INKY, USDT, or CAKE tokens and earn daily rewards. INKY Pool offers flexible, 12-month, and 36-month lock-up plans with higher APR for longer commitments. USDT and CAKE operate in flexible format only. See [Staking](/features/staking/index).

### Swap

The Swap converts assets internally through USDT as the central pair. All 8 non-USDT assets are available. In the app, users see Buy and Sell actions for each asset. Fixed US$3 fee per operation, internal execution, no gas fee. Fiat on-ramp is available on a separate screen within the Wallet. See [Swap](/features/swap).

### Arena

Inkryptus offers Coin Flip, an on-chain game where users bet INKY tokens with a 1.9x payout multiplier and a 10% platform house edge. See [Arena](/features/arena).
