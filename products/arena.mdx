The Arena is the gaming section of the Inkryptus platform. It hosts on-chain games where users wager INKY tokens and compete against the platform.

## Available Games

### Coin Flip

Coin Flip is a simple winner-take-all game:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place a bet" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    User selects a bet amount in INKY and chooses heads or tails.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bet is locked" icon="lock" title-type="p">
    The bet amount is transferred from the user's wallet to the Coin Flip contract.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Outcome determined" icon="target" title-type="p">
    A random outcome (heads or tails) is generated by the smart contract.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payout" icon="wallet" title-type="p">
    If the user wins, payout is 1.9x the bet amount (platform fee already deducted). If the user loses, the bet amount goes to the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Balance updated" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    The result is immediately reflected in the user's wallet balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Fee and payout structure

A 10% fee is embedded in the win multiplier. The player always sees the net payout (1.9x), never the gross (2.0x).

| Outcome | Player receives | Platform fee | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Win** | Bet x 1.9 (original bet + 0.9x profit) | 0.1x of the bet | Fee distributed via 30/70 commission split |
| **Loss** | 0 INKY | 100% of the bet | Full bet goes to the platform |

#### Example: 100 INKY bet, player wins

| Recipient | Amount | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Player | 190 INKY (1.9x) | Net payout, fee already deducted |
| Platform fee | 10 INKY (0.1x) | Distributed via 30/70 commission split |

The 10 INKY platform fee follows the standard commission distribution (30% Inkryptus, 70% across 7 IBO levels). See [Commissions](/partner-program/commissions) for details.

## Betting asset

All Coin Flip bets use INKY tokens. Users cannot play with USDT, BNB, or other assets. Minimum and maximum bet amounts may apply (see app for current limits). Bet amounts are denominated in INKY at the time of the bet.

## On-chain verification

<Callout kind="success">
  All Coin Flip games are executed on-chain and recorded on BNB Smart Chain. All game outcomes and transactions are recorded on BscScan, immutable once confirmed, and verifiable by reviewing smart contract events.
</Callout>

## Fair randomness

The outcome of each Coin Flip game is determined by the smart contract using a randomness source. The exact randomness mechanism (block hash, Chainlink VRF, or other method) is documented in [Contracts](/inky-token/contracts). Users should review the contract code to understand how randomness is generated.

## Responsible gaming

<Callout kind="danger">
  Coin Flip is a high-risk game where the expected value for users is negative (due to the platform fee). Users should understand the fee structure before betting, treat bets as entertainment (not investment), set personal loss limits and stick to them, and avoid chasing losses.
</Callout>
