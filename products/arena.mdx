The Arena is the gaming section of the Inkryptus platform. It hosts on-chain games where users wager INKY tokens and compete against the platform.

## Available Games

### Coin Flip

Coin Flip is a simple winner-take-all game:

1. **User places a bet**: User selects a bet amount in INKY and chooses heads or tails
2. **Bet is locked**: The bet amount is transferred from the user's wallet to the Coin Flip contract
3. **Outcome is determined on-chain**: A random outcome (heads or tails) is generated by the smart contract
4. **Winner receives payout**: If the user wins, payout is 1.9x the bet amount (platform fee already deducted). If the user loses, the bet amount goes to the platform
5. **Balance is updated**: The result is immediately reflected in the user's wallet balance

### Fee and payout structure

A 10% fee is embedded in the win multiplier. The player always sees the net payout (1.9x), never the gross (2.0x).

- **Bet loss**: 100% of the bet goes to the platform
- **Bet win**: Player receives bet x 1.9 (original bet + 0.9x profit)
- **Platform fee**: 0.1x of the bet on each win (10% of gross winnings)

#### Example: 100 INKY bet, player wins

| Recipient | Amount | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Player | 190 INKY (1.9x) | Net payout, fee already deducted |
| Platform fee | 10 INKY (0.1x) | Distributed via 30/70 commission split |

The 10 INKY platform fee follows the standard commission distribution (30% Inkryptus, 70% across 7 IBO levels). See [Commissions](../partner-program/commissions.mdx) for details.

## Betting asset

All Coin Flip bets use INKY tokens. Users cannot play with USDT, BNB, or other assets.

- Minimum and maximum bet amounts may apply (see app for current limits)
- Bet amounts are denominated in INKY at the time of the bet

## On-chain verification

All Coin Flip games are executed on-chain and recorded on BNB Smart Chain:

- **Transparent**: All game outcomes and transactions are recorded on BscScan and can be verified by users
- **Immutable**: Once a game is resolved and the transaction is confirmed, the result cannot be changed
- **Verifiable**: Users can review their complete game history by examining the smart contract events

## Fair randomness

The outcome of each Coin Flip game is determined by the smart contract using a randomness source. The exact randomness mechanism (block hash, Chainlink VRF, or other method) is documented in [Contracts](../inky-token/contracts.mdx). Users should review the contract code to understand how randomness is generated.

## Responsible gaming

Coin Flip is a high-risk game where the expected value for users is negative (due to the platform fee). Users should:

- Understand the fee structure before betting
- Treat bets as entertainment, not investment
- Set personal loss limits and stick to them
- Avoid chasing losses
