This section documents the primary products available on the Inkryptus platform. Each product includes technical details on mechanics, contract addresses, fees, and on-chain architecture.

## Product overview

### Wallet

The Inkryptus custodial wallet holds all user assets on the blockchain. Users deposit and withdraw crypto through the platform without managing private keys. Supports BTC, ETH, BNB, USDT, INKY, CAKE, and additional assets. See [Wallet](./wallet.mdx).

### Staking: INKY Pool

Users stake INKY tokens and earn INKY rewards from daily protocol emission. Three lock-up plans available: Flexible (no lock), 12-month, and 36-month, with higher APR for longer commitments. See [INKY Pool Staking](./staking/inky-pool.mdx).

### Staking: USDT

Users stake USDT and earn yield sourced from protocol revenue and platform incentives. Flexible, no lock-up required. See [USDT Staking](./staking/other-assets.mdx).

### Staking: CAKE

Users stake CAKE (PancakeSwap governance token) and earn rewards from PancakeSwap protocol incentives and platform matching. Flexible, no lock-up required. See [CAKE Staking](./staking/other-assets.mdx).

### Buy and Sell

Users purchase crypto (BTC, ETH, USDT, BNB, INKY, etc.) directly in the app using fiat payment methods, or sell crypto to fiat. Internal price quoting and execution. See [Buy and Sell](./buy-sell.mdx).

### Games

Inkryptus offers Coin Flip, an on-chain game where users bet INKY tokens with winner-take-all mechanics and a platform house edge. See [Arena](./arena.mdx).
