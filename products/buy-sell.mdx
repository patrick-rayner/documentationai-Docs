The Inkryptus platform enables users to purchase and sell cryptocurrency in multiple ways: through in-app fiat on/off-ramps, internal swaps, and external trading on PancakeSwap.

## Trading methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Fiat on-ramp" icon="credit-card">
    Users can purchase USDT directly with fiat payment methods through the Inkryptus app:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select amount" icon="circle" title-type="p">
        User selects buy amount and payment method (bank transfer, card, etc.).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive quote" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
        Platform generates a quote showing the fiat amount and USDT to be received.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete payment" icon="credit-card" title-type="p">
        User completes the fiat payment.
      </Step>
      <Step title="USDT credited" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
        Platform confirms payment receipt and executes the USDT transfer to the user's wallet.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="info">
      Fiat on-ramps require KYC verification, are subject to daily and monthly limits by KYC tier, and may take hours or days depending on the payment method. Jurisdiction-specific restrictions apply.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Fiat off-ramp" icon="banknote">
    The reverse flow is available for selling USDT to fiat:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Initiate sell" icon="circle" title-type="p">
        User initiates a sell request specifying USDT amount and withdrawal method.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive quote" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
        Platform generates a quote.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm transaction" icon="check" title-type="p">
        User confirms the transaction.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fiat payout" icon="banknote" title-type="p">
        Platform processes the USDT withdrawal and initiates fiat payout to the user's bank or payment method.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    Processing times and daily limits apply.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="In-app swap" icon="repeat">
    Users can swap between supported assets within the app. The primary swap pair is INKY/USDT, though other pairs may be available.

    - **Quote-based pricing**: The platform provides a quote before execution
    - **Direct execution**: Swaps are executed by the platform at the quoted price
    - **Fee structure**: See [Fees](/fees/index) for swap fee rates

    Example: A user with 1,000 INKY can receive a quote to swap to USDT, approve, and receive USDT in their wallet immediately.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="PancakeSwap" icon="arrow-right-left">
    Users who wish to trade INKY outside the Inkryptus app can use PancakeSwap v2:

    - **INKY/USDT pair**: The primary liquidity pool where INKY trades against USDT
    - **Permissionless**: Any user can send INKY to PancakeSwap and receive USDT (or vice versa)
    - **PancakeSwap fees apply**: All trades incur a 0.25% swap fee, plus gas costs on BNB Smart Chain
    - **Slippage**: Large trades relative to pool depth may experience slippage

    <Callout kind="alert">
      Users must have BNB in their wallet to cover gas fees for external swaps.
    </Callout>

    Current liquidity pool depth is published in [Liquidity](/inky-token/liquidity).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Supported assets for trading

| Asset | Available for buy | Available for sell | Swap pairs |
|-------|------|-------|-----------|
| USDT | Yes (fiat on-ramp) | Yes (fiat off-ramp) | INKY/USDT |
| INKY | Yes (from USDT swap) | Yes (to USDT swap) | INKY/USDT, others on PancakeSwap |
| BTC | Yes (from fiat or swap) | Yes (to fiat or swap) | Depends on available pairs |
| ETH | Yes (from fiat or swap) | Yes (to fiat or swap) | Depends on available pairs |
| BNB | Yes (from fiat or swap) | Yes (to fiat or swap) | Depends on available pairs |
| CAKE | Yes (from fiat or swap) | Yes (to fiat or swap) | Depends on available pairs |

## Price quoting

The platform provides real-time price quotes based on current market price (derived from INKY/USDT liquidity pool or external market data), slippage estimates for large trades, fee deductions, and a quote validity window (typically seconds to minutes). Users must confirm a quote before execution. If the market moves significantly, a new quote is required.

## Fee structure

All buy, sell, and swap operations are subject to fees. See [Fees](/fees/index) for details on fiat on/off-ramp fees, in-app swap fees, PancakeSwap trading fees (external), and network gas fees.
