The Inkryptus platform enables users to purchase and sell cryptocurrency in multiple ways: through in-app fiat on/off-ramps, internal swaps, and external trading on PancakeSwap.

## In-app fiat on-ramps (USDT purchase)

Users can purchase USDT directly with fiat payment methods through the Inkryptus app:

1. User selects buy amount and payment method (bank transfer, card, etc.)
2. Platform generates a quote showing the fiat amount and USDT to be received
3. User completes the fiat payment
4. Platform confirms payment receipt and executes the USDT transfer to the user's wallet
5. USDT balance is credited in the app

This flow is subject to:

- **KYC verification**: Users must complete identity verification to use fiat on-ramps
- **Daily and monthly limits**: Limits vary by KYC tier
- **Processing time**: Fiat payment processing may take hours or days depending on the payment method
- **Regulatory compliance**: Fiat on-ramps are subject to jurisdiction-specific restrictions

### Fiat off-ramps (USDT sale)

The reverse flow is available for selling USDT to fiat:

1. User initiates a sell request specifying USDT amount and withdrawal method
2. Platform generates a quote
3. User confirms the transaction
4. Platform processes the USDT withdrawal from the user's wallet and initiates fiat payout
5. Fiat is transferred to the user's bank or payment method

Processing times and daily limits apply.

## In-app crypto swaps

Users can swap between supported assets within the app. The primary swap pair is INKY/USDT, though other pairs may be available.

- **Quote-based pricing**: The platform provides a quote before execution
- **Direct execution**: Swaps are executed by the platform at the quoted price
- **Fee structure**: See [Fees and Commissions](../fees/) for swap fee rates

Example: A user with 1,000 INKY can receive a quote to swap to USDT, approve, and receive USDT in their wallet immediately.

## External trading on PancakeSwap

Users who wish to trade INKY outside the Inkryptus app can use their custodial wallet balance to interact with PancakeSwap v2:

- **INKY/USDT pair**: The primary liquidity pool on PancakeSwap where INKY trades against USDT
- **Permissionless**: Any user can send INKY from their Inkryptus wallet to PancakeSwap and receive USDT (or vice versa)
- **PancakeSwap fees apply**: All trades incur a 0.25% swap fee, plus gas costs on BNB Smart Chain
- **Slippage**: Large trades relative to pool depth may experience slippage

Users must have BNB in their wallet to cover gas fees for external swaps.

Current liquidity pool depth is published in [Liquidity](../inky-token/liquidity.mdx).

## Fee structure

All buy, sell, and swap operations are subject to fees. See [Fees and Commissions](../fees/) for details on:

- Fiat on/off-ramp fees
- In-app swap fees
- PancakeSwap trading fees (external)
- Network gas fees (for external transactions)

## Price quoting

The platform provides real-time price quotes based on:

- **Current market price**: Derived from INKY/USDT liquidity pool or external market data
- **Slippage estimates**: For large trades relative to available liquidity
- **Fee deduction**: Quotes are net of applicable fees
- **Quote validity window**: Quotes are typically valid for a short time (seconds to minutes) before expiring

Users must confirm a quote before execution. If the market moves significantly, a new quote is required.
