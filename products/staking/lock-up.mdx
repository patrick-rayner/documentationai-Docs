title: Lock-up Plans

Staking plans on Inkryptus can be either flexible (no lock-up) or term-based (with lock-up of principal).

## Plans and lock-up periods

| Plan | Lock-up | Principal access | Profit access |
| ---- | ------- | ---------------- | ------------- |
| Flexible (Unlocked) | None | Withdraw anytime | Harvest anytime (after 24h) |
| 12 months | 12 months from activation | At maturity only | Harvest anytime (after 24h) |
| 36 months | 36 months from activation | At maturity only | Harvest anytime (after 24h) |

## Key rules

**During lock-up:**

- The staked principal cannot be withdrawn before the term ends.
- There is no early unstake mechanism. The contract runs until maturity.
- Profit can be harvested at any time after the first 24 hours.

**At maturity:**

- The full principal is released to the user's free wallet balance.
- Any remaining unharvested profit is also released.
- The contract is closed.

**Flexible plan:**

- No lock-up. The user can close the contract and withdraw the principal at any time.
- Rewards are still credited daily while the contract is active.

## Discontinued plans

The 6-month and 24-month lock-up plans were discontinued in V2.8.0 for new contracts. Existing contracts on those terms continue running normally until their maturity date.

## USDT and CAKE staking

USDT and CAKE staking products operate exclusively in Flexible format (no lock-up). See [Other assets](./other-assets.mdx).
