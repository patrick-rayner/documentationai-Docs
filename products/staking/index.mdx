Inkryptus offers staking products across multiple assets on BNB Smart Chain. Users deposit tokens into smart contracts and receive daily rewards based on the specific product's mechanics.

## Available products

| Product | Assets | Format | Rewards | Contract |
| ------- | ------ | ------ | ------- | -------- |
| [INKY Pool](/products/staking/inky-pool) | INKY | Flexible, 12 months, 36 months | Daily, in INKY (emission-based) | [`0x9b8eC6ac...`](https://bscscan.com/address/0x9b8eC6ac014b926201f085e53A0d0540F7C510c5#code) |

| [CAKE Smart Farm](/products/staking/other-assets#cake) | CAKE | Flexible | Daily, in CAKE | Managed via Products Vault |

  BSW (Biswap) staking has been discontinued.
</Callout>

## Common rules

All staking products share these properties:

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Performance fee** | 25% on profit only. No profit, no fee. The fee never applies to the principal |
| **Harvest fee** | Flat US$3 per operation (same as Swap and Withdraw) |
| **Harvest availability** | 24 hours after contract activation |
| **Network** | BNB Smart Chain |
| **Custody** | Inkryptus is a custodial platform. User balances are managed inside platform contracts |
