## Overview

INKY is the utility token of the Inkryptus ecosystem. It runs on BNB Smart Chain under the BEP-20 standard and has been live since May 2023. This documentation is the technical source of truth for everything related to INKY: supply, emission, fees, liquidity, security, and contract architecture.

All commercial narrative and educational material lives elsewhere:

- Commercial pages: `/inky-token/` and `/inky-token/tokenomics/`
- Educational hub: `/learn/tokenomics/` and related `/learn/` articles

This section focuses exclusively on verifiable, on-chain facts and technical parameters.
