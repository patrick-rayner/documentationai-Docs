## Initial allocation

At deploy, 10,000,000 INKY were minted and sent to the Initial Mintage Vault ([`0x19d24adf08d2c169667353957adb02e7aa4127a9`](https://bscscan.com/address/0x19d24adf08d2c169667353957adb02e7aa4127a9#code)). This vault releases tokens according to the Inkryptus economic plan.

The detailed breakdown of how the initial 10M was allocated across categories (team, reserves, liquidity seed, etc.) is not disclosed publicly.

## Current distribution

The on-chain distribution of INKY can be tracked through BscScan:

- [INKY holders page](https://bscscan.com/token/0x75a320c97205dd2e70e09085d1408c73a73d4d8f#balances)

Key system addresses that hold INKY in non-circulating roles are listed in [Supply](/inky-token/supply) and [Contracts](/inky-token/contracts).

## Ongoing emission

New tokens enter circulation daily through the staking mechanism. See [Emission](/inky-token/emission) for the formula, rate, and cap.
