## On-chain token fees

The INKY token contract does **not** apply any fee on buy, sell, or transfer operations. When a user buys, sells, or transfers INKY, the only cost is the standard BNB Smart Chain gas fee.

There is no buy tax, sell tax, or transfer tax embedded in the token contract.

## Platform fees

Fees charged by the Inkryptus platform (swap spreads, staking performance fees, product access fees) are separate from the token contract and are documented in the [Fees & Commissions](../fees/index.mdx) section.

The staking performance fee (25% on profit) is applied at the product level by the staking contract, not at the token transfer level. See [Emission](./emission.mdx) for details.

## How to verify

Open the [INKY contract source on BscScan](https://bscscan.com/address/0x75a320c97205dd2e70e09085d1408c73a73d4d8f#code) and inspect the `transfer` and `transferFrom` functions. No fee logic is present in the token contract itself.
