## On-chain token fees

<Callout kind="success">
  The INKY token contract does **not** apply any fee on buy, sell, or transfer operations. When a user buys, sells, or transfers INKY, the only cost is the standard BNB Smart Chain gas fee.
</Callout>

There is no buy tax, sell tax, or transfer tax embedded in the token contract.

## Platform fees

Fees charged by the Inkryptus platform (swap spreads, staking performance fees, product access fees) are separate from the token contract and are documented in the [Fees](/fees/index) section.

The staking performance fee (25% on profit) is applied at the product level by the staking contract, not at the token transfer level. See [Emission](/inky-token/emission) for details.

## How to verify

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open contract source" icon="code" title-type="p">
    Open the [INKY contract source on BscScan](https://bscscan.com/address/0x75a320c97205dd2e70e09085d1408c73a73d4d8f#code).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspect transfer functions" icon="search" title-type="p">
    Inspect the `transfer` and `transferFrom` functions. No fee logic is present in the token contract itself.
  </Step>
</Steps>
