## How new INKY tokens enter circulation

All new INKY tokens are created through the staking mechanism. There is no other emission source. The staking pool contract ([`0x9b8eC6ac...`](https://bscscan.com/address/0x9b8eC6ac014b926201f085e53A0d0540F7C510c5#code)) handles emission and uses the **TokenMinter** ([`0x68bdab3d...`](https://bscscan.com/address/0x68bdab3dcc5332bcccdc940d54122c155b80857a)) to call the INKY contract's mint function. Current staked volume is publicly verifiable on BscScan.

## Emission formula

The daily emission follows two rules applied simultaneously:

1. **Rate**: 1% of the total INKY currently staked across all plans.
2. **Cap**: maximum of 10,000 INKY per day, regardless of staked volume.

Whichever value is lower applies. The emitted tokens are distributed proportionally among all staking participants based on their individual share of the total staked amount.

## Performance fee

<Callout kind="info">
  A **25% performance fee** is applied daily on staking profits. This fee is collected by the platform from the rewards distributed to each participant. See [Performance Fee](/introduction/fees#performance-fee) for details.
</Callout>

## Parameter governance

The emission rate (1%) and daily cap (10,000 INKY) are configurable functions in the staking contract. Changes to these parameters require on-chain execution by the contract owner.

A **TokenMinter** contract ([`0x68bdab3d...`](https://bscscan.com/address/0x68bdab3dcc5332bcccdc940d54122c155b80857a)) manages which addresses can call the mint function. The Initial Mintage Vault uses a multisig arrangement for fund movements. See [Contracts](/inky-token/contracts) for the full architecture.

No emission parameter has been changed since deploy.
