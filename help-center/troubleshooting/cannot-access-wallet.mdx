description: Quick fix for access problems.
---

## Issue

- Unable to log in to wallet
- Page displays "Invalid password" or "Wallet not found"

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore with recovery phrase">
    Choose “Restore Wallet” and enter your secure recovery phrase.
  </Step>
</Steps>
