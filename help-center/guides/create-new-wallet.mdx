## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Wallets">
    Log in on `dashboard.inkryptus.com`. Click the “Wallets” tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Wallet">
    Press “Create Wallet”. Choose a name and set a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backup">
    Download your recovery phrase. Store it in a safe place. You’ll need it to restore access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete">
    Your wallet is ready for transfers and deposits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
