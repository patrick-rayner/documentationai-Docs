## Create an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app" icon="download" title-type="p">
    Get the official Inkryptus app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start registration" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Tap **Create Account / Sign Up** on the welcome screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter email, full name, username, and referral code (if applicable).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox for the verification code and enter it in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set password and complete" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Create a strong password, complete profile details, and accept the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Expected result:** account created and ready for immediate use.

<Callout kind="info">
  If you did not enter a referral code during sign-up, you have up to 30 days to add a Sponsor in `Profile > Account > Sponsor`.
</Callout>

---

## Reset password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open recovery" icon="key" title-type="p">
    On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Enter your registered email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate code" icon="check" title-type="p">
    Check your inbox, copy the verification code, and enter it in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set new password" icon="lock" title-type="p">
    Create a new password and finish the reset process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Biometric authentication (Face ID / Fingerprint)

<Steps>
  <Step title="First login prompt" icon="smartphone" title-type="p">
    On first login, enable biometrics when the app prompts you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manual toggle" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    After that: `Profile > Security > Biometric Login`. To disable, return to the same menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Complete KYC (identity verification)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open verification" icon="user" title-type="p">
    After login, tap the profile icon, go to **Security**, then **Identity Verification**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose document" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Select a valid ID (passport, national ID, or driver's license).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Photograph document" icon="camera" title-type="p">
    Follow instructions to capture front and back. Ensure the image is clear, without reflections, and all information is readable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Selfie validation" icon="smile" title-type="p">
    Position your face within the indicated frame for facial verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and wait" icon="clock" title-type="p">
    Submit files and wait for analysis. Standard timeframe is up to 2 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  If you receive a notification about data mismatch, redo the process with clearer images.
</Callout>
