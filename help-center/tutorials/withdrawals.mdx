## Send assets (external withdrawal)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select asset" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    Open the asset in your wallet and tap **Send**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste destination" icon="copy" title-type="p">
    Paste the destination wallet address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm BEP20" icon="check" title-type="p">
    Verify that the destination wallet supports BNB Smart Chain (BEP20).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter the withdrawal amount. Observe the minimum (US$6) and the fixed network fee (US$3).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate with email code" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    Tap **Confirm** and enter the security code sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track transaction" icon="activity" title-type="p">
    Monitor progress through the transaction hash in app history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="warning">
  Before confirming, review address, asset, and BEP20 network. Send a test transaction with a small amount when needed.
</Callout>

---

## How to find the TXID (hash)

- **External transfers:** open asset details and locate the transaction hash.
- **Internal actions:** check the transaction ID in app history.
- If you cannot find it, contact support with operation details.

## Example: Send USDT to Binance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Binance address" icon="copy" title-type="p">
    In Binance: **Wallet > Assets > Add Funds > Deposit Crypto**. Select USDT, choose BSC (BEP20), and copy the deposit address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send from Inkryptus" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
    In Inkryptus: **Wallet > Assets > Tether (USDT) > Send**. Paste the Binance address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount and confirm" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Tap **Next**, enter amount (or MAX), tap **Send**, and validate with email code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Expected result:** USDT arrives in Binance for conversion to local currency.

---

## Withdraw to local currency (off-ramp via Binance)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm receipt" icon="check" title-type="p">
    In Binance, verify USDT arrived in **Wallet > Spot**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert to fiat" icon="dollar-sign" title-type="p">
    Convert USDT to your local currency (BRL, USD, EUR, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
    Go to **Wallet > Spot > Withdraw** and choose your method (PIX, SEPA, etc.).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Binance may require KYC and additional checks depending on your region.
</Callout>

## Withdrawal limits

| Tier | KYC Status | Monthly Limit |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Bronze | No KYC | US$500/month |
| Silver | KYC verified | US$500,000/month |

The counter resets on the 1st of each month at 00:00:01 UTC. Check your current limits in `Profile > Account > Fees`.
