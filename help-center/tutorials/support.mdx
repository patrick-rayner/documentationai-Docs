## Support channels

| Channel | How to access |
|---------|-------------|
| In-app ticket | `Home > Support > Open a New Ticket` |
| Email | Send to support@inkryptus.com (ticket created automatically) |
| AI chat | `Home > Support > Chat with Support` |

**Recommended path:** open via app first, then follow updates by email.

---

## Manual ticket flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ticket" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    Go to **Home > Support > Open a New Ticket**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow up by email" icon="mail" title-type="p">
    After opening, monitor and reply through your registered email inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach evidence" icon="paperclip" title-type="p">
    If the case requires screenshots, documents, or proof, send them in the same email thread.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Keep the same email thread to avoid duplicated tickets and delayed handling.
</Callout>

---

## AI-assisted support (Chat with Support)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open chat" icon="message-circle" title-type="p">
    Go to **Home > Support > Chat with Support**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the issue" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Explain the problem and follow the AI guidance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalate if needed" icon="arrow-up" title-type="p">
    If the AI cannot resolve it, request: "create a ticket". The AI sends conversation context and opens the ticket automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Ticket quality checklist

Always include as much information as possible when opening a ticket:

| Field | When to include |
|-------|----------------|
| Transaction ID (TXID/hash) | Transfers, swap, withdrawal, or deposit issues |
| Problem description | What happened, expected behavior, current status |
| Attempted solution | What you already tried before contacting support |
| Date/time (with timezone) | Always |
| Asset and network | Always |
| Evidence | Screenshots, error messages, relevant account data (without sensitive info) |

**Example (deposit not received):** include TXID, deposit address, sent asset/network, exact date/time, amount, and screenshot of the sending wallet or exchange.
