## Request your Account Report

The Account Report is a complete record of your platform transactions, useful for asset and tax declarations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Statement" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Go to `Profile > Account > Statement`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select period" icon="calendar" title-type="p">
    Choose the date range you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request report" icon="download" title-type="p">
    Tap to request. The report will be sent to your registered email within 24h.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Inkryptus does not provide tax advice or submit filings on your behalf. Tax responsibility rests solely with the user.
</Callout>
