<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Buy USDT with fiat" icon="credit-card">
    The fastest way to fund your account if you do not already have crypto in an external wallet.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Add Funds" icon="plus" title-type="p">
        On the Home screen, tap **Add Funds**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Buy USDT" icon="dollar-sign" title-type="p">
        Choose **Buy USDT** from the options.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose provider" icon="credit-card" title-type="p">
        Select your preferred payment provider from the list.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter amount and confirm" icon="check" title-type="p">
        Enter the purchase amount, tap **Confirm**, then **Continue to payment**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete payment" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
        Allow GPS access if prompted (required by provider). Complete the payment inside the provider's screen with your card or available method.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive USDT" icon="download" title-type="p">
        Once the provider confirms, USDT is deposited automatically into your Inkryptus account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="info">
      Availability may vary by region. If no provider is available in your country, use the crypto deposit method.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Deposit crypto" icon="download">
    Generate your unique deposit address to receive supported assets from an external wallet or exchange.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open deposit" icon="plus" title-type="p">
        In the app, tap **Add Funds > Deposit Crypto**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select asset" icon="circle" title-type="p">
        Choose the asset you want to receive (e.g., USDT, BTCB, INKY).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy address" icon="copy" title-type="p">
        Tap **Copy Address** or scan the QR code displayed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send from external source" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
        In your external wallet or exchange, paste the address and select **BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)** as the network.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="warning">
      Sending on the wrong network will result in permanent loss of funds. Always confirm BEP20 before proceeding.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Example: via Binance" icon="building">
    Step-by-step example depositing USDT from Binance into Inkryptus.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Prepare on Binance" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
        On Binance, buy or convert your balance to USDT.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Get Inkryptus address" icon="copy" title-type="p">
        In the Inkryptus app: **Add Funds > Deposit Crypto > USDT**. Copy the address.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Withdraw from Binance" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
        In Binance withdrawal, paste the address and select **BSC (BEP20)** as the network.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
        Enter the amount, confirm security checks, and wait for processing.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="info">
      For first-time transfers, send a small test amount to verify the flow.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Check deposit status

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Crypto deposit" icon="link">
    Check the transaction status on the sending platform. BEP20 confirmations are usually fast but may take a few minutes.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Fiat purchase" icon="credit-card">
    Tap **Add Funds > Buy USDT > History** tab to see all past transactions and their status (pending, completed, failed).

    If a purchase shows as completed in History but the balance has not updated yet, wait a few minutes and refresh the app.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Deposit not reflected after 24h" default-open="false">
    In some cases, deposits can take up to 24 hours. If it takes longer, contact support with all transaction details: TXID, asset, amount, and date/time.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Safety tips" default-open="false">
    - Always verify the first and last 5-6 characters of the destination address before confirming.
    - Minimum deposit depends on the platform or provider used.
    - When in doubt, send a small test transaction first.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
