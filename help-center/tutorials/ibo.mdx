## Entry point

Path: **Home > IBO Program**. This area centralizes dashboard metrics, referral links, and operational IBO details.

The dashboard is organized into four sections: **Subscription**, **Earnings**, **Network**, and **Wallet**.

---

## First-time onboarding

The first time you open IBO Program, a 3-screen modal explains how the module works. This modal appears only once (first access).

---

## Subscription screen

- Shows current subscription status.
- Provides the **Renew Subscription** action. Renewal is manual, paid in USDT.
- Displays your **Invite Link** and a scannable **QR Code**.

<Callout kind="info">
  Invite links are mobile-only. If opened on desktop, the user is redirected to the Inkryptus website.
</Callout>

---

## Earnings screen

- Detailed earnings panel with date filters.
- History of earnings and level-by-level breakdown.
- Data refresh: once per day at **00:30 UTC**.

---

## Network screen

- Quantitative network metrics (total users in your network).
- Growth indicator compared to the last 7 days.
- At the bottom, enter a username to verify whether that user belongs to your network.
- Registration events appear in **real-time** (unlike Earnings, which update daily).

---

## IBO Wallet

This is the most important IBO tab. IBO-related earnings are separated from your main wallet.

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| Transfer | Moves all commission and campaign balances from IBO Wallet to your User Wallet (currently no fee). |
| Harvest | Moves balances related to profit fee only (US$3 fixed fee applies; amounts below US$3 cannot be withdrawn). |

**Estimated Balance** shows the total currently available for withdrawal or transfer.

---

## Balance detail and history

- Asset-by-asset balance details inside IBO Wallet.
- History report of transfers from IBO Wallet to User Wallet.
- Use this report for reconciliation of commissions, fees, and payout operations.

---

## Sponsor and contact sharing

Your **username** is always shared automatically with referred users.

Additional contact data is opt-in (none shared by default):

| Data | Default |
|------|---------|
| Username | Always visible |
| Name | Not shared |
| Email | Not shared |
| Phone | Not shared |

Configure what you share in `Profile > Account > Sponsor`.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Fast Bonus was discontinued on 24 April 2026 (v2.8.0).
</Callout>
