---
title: Swap (Buy & Sell)
description: "How to buy and sell assets inside the Inkryptus app using USDT as the reference pair."
---

In the app, the reference asset is always **USDT**. All buy/sell operations route through USDT.

---

## Buy an asset (e.g., INKY)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open asset" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    In the **Assets** tab, tap the asset you want to buy (e.g., INKY).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Buy" icon="arrow-down" title-type="p">
    Select the **Buy** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter the USDT amount. The system calculates the token quantity based on current price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Review the fee and minimum shown on screen, then confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Sell an asset

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open asset" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    Select the asset you hold and tap **Sell**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter quantity" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter the amount of the asset to sell.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review estimate vs. execution" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    The first screen shows an **initial estimate** based on current price. On the confirmation step, the app queries the contract and shows the **real execution amount**, considering liquidity impact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Review the real amount and all fees on the confirmation screen, then approve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  The decision value is always the confirmation-screen amount, which includes pool liquidity impact.
</Callout>

---

## Typical flow

```
USDT → Buy INKY → Stake → Harvest → Sell INKY → USDT → Withdraw
```

---

## Important notes

- A fixed fee of **US$3** applies per swap operation.
- For external withdrawals, convert INKY to USDT before sending. Not all exchanges/wallets recognize INKY.
- There are no direct routes between non-USDT assets. To move between two assets (e.g., INKY to BTCB), sell the first for USDT, then buy the second with USDT.
