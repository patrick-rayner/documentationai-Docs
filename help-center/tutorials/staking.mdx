## Cycle rule

In Inkryptus Staking, operations run in **cycles**. Relevant actions (open, close, harvest) take up to **1 cycle** to settle. On average, consider **24h** per cycle.

---

## Open a new contract

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Staking" icon="trending-up" title-type="p">
    Go to **Products > Staking** (or the **Stake** shortcut on Home).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select product" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    Choose the desired staking product (e.g., INKY Pool 36 months or USDT Staking).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open contract" icon="plus" title-type="p">
    Tap **Open a new contract**, enter the amount, review estimated yield and lock rules, then confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation" icon="clock" title-type="p">
    Contract opening takes up to 1 cycle. After confirmation, first rewards are generated in the next cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Fee:** there is no fee to open a contract.

---

## Harvest (claim rewards)

Harvest means claiming rewards without closing the main contract.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select contract" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Open the active contract that has available profit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Harvest" icon="download" title-type="p">
    Tap **Harvest**, review fees, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive funds" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    Harvested value is credited to your available Assets balance in up to 1 cycle (average 24h).
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Harvest fee:** fixed US$3 charged at claim time. **Minimum harvest amount:** US$3.

---

## Close a contract

To redeem your principal (invested capital), you must close the contract.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select contract" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Select the target contract in your products list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request close" icon="x-circle" title-type="p">
    Tap **Close contract**. Fixed-term contracts can be closed at maturity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive principal" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    After the close request, principal is released to your wallet in up to 1 cycle (average 24h).
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Fee:** there is no fee to close a contract.

---

## Common cycle questions

- **No yield yet after opening:** expected. Opening confirms in up to 1 cycle, first reward comes in the next cycle.
- **Harvest balance not credited instantly:** expected. Credit happens in up to 1 cycle.
- **Funds not returned after closing:** expected. Settlement happens in up to 1 cycle after the request.
- **Reduce mistakes:** always check staking product type, confirmed amount, contract status, and history before opening a support ticket.
