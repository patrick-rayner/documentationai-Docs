## How it works

Coin Flip is an on-chain utility using INKY.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a side" icon="circle" title-type="p">
    Select **Heads** or **Tails**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set amount" icon="edit" title-type="p">
    Enter the INKY amount you want to play with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review payout" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Check the payout displayed on screen before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play" icon="zap" title-type="p">
    Tap **Play** and get the result instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  This feature is intended for users aged 18+. Use only amounts compatible with your profile and strategy.
</Callout>
