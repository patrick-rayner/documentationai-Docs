## Home screen

The Home screen shows your estimated balance (assets + products) and provides quick access to core actions.

**Shortcuts:** Buy INKY, INKY Pool, IBO Program, and Coin Flip.

**Main tabs:** Assets and Stake.

---

## Wallet > Overview

Total estimated balance and composition chart. Quick access to **Add Funds** and **Send**.

---

## Wallet > Assets

Lists all available assets and balances by token. Each asset has its own screen with actions: deposit, send, buy, and sell (when applicable).

---

## Wallet > Products

Shows your staking contracts with average APR, all-time profit, and active balances.

---

## Profile > Account

The v2.8.0 update centralized key settings in `Profile > Account`:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Fees | Centralized view of all platform fees and current withdrawal limits by tier. |
| Statement | Request your Account Report (transaction history for tax support). |
| Sponsor | Add or view the linked Sponsor. For IBOs, configure contact sharing (opt-in). |
| KYC | Start or track identity verification. |
