---
title: Account & Security
description: "Questions about account creation, password recovery, KYC verification, and platform security features."
---

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I create an account?" default-open="true">
    Download the official app, tap **Create Account / Sign Up**, fill in the requested information (including referral code, if you have one), validate the code sent by email, create your password, and accept the terms. Registration is available only for users aged 18+.

    If you do not enter a referral code during sign-up, you have up to **30 days** to add a Sponsor in `Profile > Account > Sponsor`. After this period, no Sponsor can be added.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="I forgot my password. What should I do?" default-open="false">
    On the login screen, use **Forgot Password**, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions sent. This flow also helps identify your username.

    If needed, contact support with your account name, registered email, details, and a screenshot of the issue.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I complete KYC (identity verification)?" default-open="false">
    After login, tap the profile icon, go to **Security**, then **Identity Verification**. Submit a valid document and a clear selfie for validation. To speed up approval, use good lighting and readable images.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Does the platform have security features?" default-open="false">
    Yes. The platform combines in-app validations, email verification for critical actions, and optional biometric authentication on the device (Face ID / Fingerprint), focused on simple usage and operational protection.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="I do not receive the verification code by email. What should I do?" default-open="false">
    Confirm your app is up to date and check inbox/spam. If the code still does not arrive, contact support with account name, registered email, details, and a screenshot of the issue.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="My KYC stayed 'under review' for too long. What is the expected timeframe?" default-open="false">
    The standard timeframe is up to 2 business days, since validation is performed by a specialized partner. If it exceeds this period, contact support with account name, email, details, and screenshot.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="KYC fails or does not start after tapping 'Start Verification'. How do I fix it?" default-open="false">
    Update the app and try again. Then check connection and camera permissions. If it persists, contact support with account name, email, details, and screenshot of the issue.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I change account email, phone, name, or birth date?" default-open="false">
    At this time, these changes are not yet enabled in the standard flow. This functionality is part of the platform roadmap and will be available in a future update.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
