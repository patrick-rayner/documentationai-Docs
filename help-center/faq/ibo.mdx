---

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What is the IBO Program?" default-open="true">
    IBO (Independent Business Owner) is the Inkryptus Partner Program. With it, participants activate a subscription, share invites, and track network and results in a dedicated dashboard.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I activate or renew?" default-open="false">
    In app, go to **IBO Program** and activate/renew manually. The subscription is not automatic.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I get my invite link/referral code?" default-open="false">
    The invite link is inside the IBO Program. After activation, it appears on the dashboard for sharing with your network. To get the link, IBO must be activated in the app.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How much does it cost, how do I pay, and how do I cancel?" default-open="false">
    Monthly subscription is US$24, paid in **USDT** directly through the IBO Program dashboard in the app. Renewal is **manual** each cycle. To stop, simply do not renew in the next cycle. The first 30 days are free.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What can I see in the IBO dashboard?" default-open="false">
    Network management indicators: team size, daily profit, all-time profit, invite link, and network activities.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How does referral work?" default-open="false">
    Share your invite link. When a new user signs up through the link, they enter your network. For the referral to be effective, the IBO must have an **active subscription** at the time of the referral's registration. IBO accounts without active subscription do not receive new users in their network.

    If a user registers without a Sponsor, they have **30 days** from account creation to add one manually in `Profile > Account > Sponsor`. After this period, no Sponsor can be added.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are commissions retroactive for users registered without a Sponsor?" default-open="false">
    No. Commissions generated before the Sponsor linkage is established are forfeited to Inkryptus. Only commissions earned after the link is created are credited to the IBO.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I share my contact details with my network?" default-open="false">
    Your username is always shared automatically with referred users. Additionally, you can choose to share name, email, and/or phone (opt-in). By default, no additional data is shared. Configure in `Profile > Account > Sponsor`.
  </Expandable>

  <Callout kind="alert">
    Fast Bonus was discontinued on 24 April 2026 (v2.8.0).
  </Callout>
</ExpandableGroup>
