## Overview

To start using Inkryptus, you need a secure wallet.

## Steps

1. Sign up at `https://dashboard.inkryptus.com`
2. Click on "Create Wallet"
3. Set a strong password
4. Download your recovery phrase (store securely)
5. Access your wallet from the dashboard

## Tip

Never share your recovery phrase. Inkryptus can’t recover it for you.
