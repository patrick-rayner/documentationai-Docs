<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What is Inkryptus?" default-open="true">
    Inkryptus is a crypto solutions platform with a digital wallet and integrated products inside the same app. In one environment, you can store assets, add funds, buy and sell, join staking, use the Partner Program, and access on-chain utilities such as Coin Flip.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Does Inkryptus work like a traditional exchange?" default-open="false">
    The model is different. Inkryptus prioritizes a wallet-first experience with native app products, reducing technical steps and making operations clearer for beginner and intermediate users.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I use it in a browser?" default-open="false">
    At the moment, primary usage is through the official iOS and Android app, where the complete operational flow of the platform is available.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
