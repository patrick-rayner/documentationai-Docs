<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Which network does Inkryptus support?" default-open="true">
    Inkryptus operates exclusively on **BNB Smart Chain (BEP20)**. Transfers on any other network may cause permanent loss of funds.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Which assets are currently supported?" default-open="false">
    The wallet works with a BEP20 asset selection aligned with the platform's current ecosystem:

    | Asset | Symbol |
    |-------|--------|
    | Dollar Tether | USDT |
    | Bitcoin (BEP-20) | BTCB |
    | Ethereum | ETH |
    | Inkryptus | INKY |
    | PancakeSwap | CAKE |
    | Biswap | BSW |
    | Radiant | RDNT |
    | Shiba Inu | SHIB |

    This list may evolve with the product roadmap. Always confirm in the app before transferring.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I send any BEP20 token?" default-open="false">
    No. In addition to being on the correct network, the asset must be listed as supported by Inkryptus. Unsupported tokens may not be credited.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
