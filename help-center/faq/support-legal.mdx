  <Expandable title="Is Inkryptus legally compliant?" default-open="false">
    The platform operates focused on security, transparency, and compliance, with continuous monitoring of requirements applicable to the digital asset sector. User eligibility and obligations depend on local jurisdiction. For legal or tax decisions, consulting a specialized professional is recommended.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I get an Account Report (statement)?" default-open="false">
    Go to `Profile > Account > Statement`, select the desired period, and request the report. It will be sent to your registered email within 24h. The Account Report is useful for supporting asset and tax declarations.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Do I need to report crypto assets?" default-open="false">
    Crypto tax rules vary by country. In many jurisdictions, you may need to report gains, losses, or income from crypto on your annual tax return, but exact rules, thresholds, and forms depend on where you live and your personal situation.

    The Inkryptus app does not provide tax advice and does not automatically report information to tax authorities. You are responsible for understanding and complying with the tax obligations that apply to you.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
