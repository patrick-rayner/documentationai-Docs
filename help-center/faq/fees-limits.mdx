---
title: Fees & Limits
description: "Questions about platform fees, transaction costs, withdrawal limits, and performance fees."
---

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What are the main operational fees?" default-open="true">
    Inkryptus applies fees by action type:

    - **Transactional fee:** US$3 per applicable operation (withdraw, harvest, swap).
    - **IBO:** monthly subscription of US$24.
    - **Staking:** performance fee charged on effective profit.

    All applicable costs are shown before confirmation. Final values may vary by product, asset, and operational updates.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I check all platform fees?" default-open="false">
    In `Profile > Account > Fees` you can find the centralized breakdown of all fees and current withdrawal limits.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are there withdrawal limits?" default-open="false">
    Yes. Limits are defined by tier:

    | Tier | KYC Status | Monthly Limit |
    |------|-----------|---------------|
    | Bronze | No KYC | US$500/month |
    | Silver | KYC verified | US$500,000/month |

    The counter resets on the 1st of each month at 00:00:01 UTC. To upgrade from Bronze to Silver, complete KYC in `Profile > Account > KYC`.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Is there a performance fee in staking?" default-open="false">
    Yes. Performance fee is **25% on effective profit** only. It is never charged on the principal amount invested. If there is no profit in the cycle, there is no charge. The remaining 75% of profit stays with the user.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Are minimum values fixed?" default-open="false">
    No. Minimums and operational conditions may vary by asset and product. Always validate the fields and warnings shown in the app before confirming.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
