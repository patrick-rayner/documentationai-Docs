This page explains the core mechanics of the Inkryptus platform: how users deposit assets, how the custodial wallet operates, what products are available, and how staking rewards are generated.

## Custodial model

Inkryptus operates a custodial platform. When a user creates an account, the platform:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account creation" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Creates an account record in the backend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wallet generation" icon="wallet" title-type="p">
    Generates an individual wallet address for the user, managed within the platform's main multisig wallet structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Balance tracking" icon="database" title-type="p">
    Tracks the user's balance through the backend database.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each user has their own wallet address on-chain. The keys for all user wallets are managed by the platform's main multisig wallet. Users do not generate, export, or manage private keys or seedphrases. This simplifies onboarding and eliminates seedphrase recovery flows, but requires users to trust the platform as a custodian.

### Staking

Inkryptus offers three staking products:

- **INKY Pool**: Stake INKY tokens and earn INKY rewards from protocol emissions. Rewards are generated by the TokenMinter contract based on a programmed emission schedule.
- **USDT Staking**: Stake USDT and earn yield sourced from protocol revenue and lending arrangements.
- **CAKE Staking**: Stake CAKE (PancakeSwap governance token) and earn rewards from PancakeSwap protocol incentives.

Users select a pool in the app, deposit their balance, and accrue rewards daily. Rewards can be harvested (claimed) at any time. There is no auto-compound; harvest is manual. See [Staking](/products/staking/index) for detailed mechanics.

### Buy and Sell

Users buy and sell crypto against USDT inside the app. USDT is the central pair for all operations: to buy an asset, the user spends USDT; to sell, the user receives USDT. There are no direct routes between non-USDT assets. The platform quotes a price, shows the fee (US$3 fixed), and executes internally with no gas fee. Fiat on/off-ramp (local currency to USDT) is available on a separate screen. See [Buy and Sell](/products/buy-sell) for details.

### Arena

Inkryptus operates a Coin Flip game where users can stake a balance and win or lose against the platform. Payouts and house edge are defined by smart contract logic. See [Arena](/products/arena) for rules and fee structure.

## On-chain infrastructure

### INKY Token

The INKY token (contract `0x75a320c97205dd2e70e09085d1408c73a73d4d8f`) is a BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain. It serves as the reward token for staking pools and platform governance. INKY has a hard cap of 200,000,000 tokens. Daily emission follows a dual rule: 1% of total staked or 10,000 INKY, whichever is lower.

All INKY transactions and balances are public on BscScan. See [INKY Token](/inky-token/index) for supply, emission, liquidity, and security details.

### Smart contracts

Inkryptus maintains three primary smart contracts on BNB Smart Chain:

| Contract | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **INKY Token** (BEP-20) | Token contract managing supply, transfers, and permissions |
| **Staking Pool** (INKY) | Handles deposits, reward accrual, and claims for INKY stakers |
| **TokenMinter** | Emits INKY rewards according to a programmed schedule |

Additional contracts manage USDT and CAKE staking. All contract addresses and ABIs are published in the [Contracts](/inky-token/contracts) section. Contract code is publicly viewable on BscScan.

### Liquidity and trading

INKY maintains liquidity on PancakeSwap v2 in the INKY/USDT pair. This allows users to swap INKY on the open market at market rates. The platform may provide or withdraw liquidity at its discretion. See [Liquidity](/inky-token/liquidity) for current pool depth and fee information.

## Reward generation

### INKY staking rewards

Users who stake INKY earn INKY rewards. Rewards are generated by the TokenMinter contract, which emits INKY daily following a dual rule: 1% of total staked or 10,000 INKY cap, whichever is lower. Emitted tokens are distributed proportionally among stakers.

<Callout kind="info">
  A 25% performance fee is applied daily on staking profits. See [Performance Fee](/fees/performance) for details.
</Callout>

### USDT and CAKE staking rewards

- **USDT**: Users lock USDT on the platform, injecting liquidity into the INKY/USDT pool. The platform allocates a portion of the daily INKY emission to reward USDT stakers, converted into USDT-denominated rewards.
- **CAKE**: An encapsulated protocol from PancakeSwap. The platform abstracts the interaction with PancakeSwap's native staking contracts. Rewards come directly from PancakeSwap's reward mechanics.

All rewards are credited daily and can be claimed via the Harvest function. Harvest is manual (no auto-compound). Each Harvest operation has a flat fee of US$3.
